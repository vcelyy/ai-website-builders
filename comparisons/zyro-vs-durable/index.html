Frequently Asked Questions

Is Zyro or Durable better for AI features?

Durable has more comprehensive AI features including AI Copywriter for landing pages, blogs, emails, and social media, plus a built-in CRM for customer management. Zyro has excellent AI tools too - AI Writer for content and unique AI Heatmap for layout optimization. For pure content generation, Durable wins. For layout optimization insights, Zyro's Heatmap is unique and useful.

Which is cheaper, Zyro or Durable?

Zyro is significantly cheaper at $3-15/month compared to Durable's flat $15/month. However, Durable includes built-in CRM which would cost extra with other builders. If you need customer relationship management, Durable delivers better value at $15/month. If budget is tight and you just need a website, Zyro at $3/month is unbeatable.

What's the main difference between Zyro and Durable?

Zyro is a website builder with AI features - marketing tools focused on content generation and layout optimization. Durable is a complete business platform - website builder plus CRM, invoicing, and lead management. Zyro is cheaper but just a website. Durable costs more but replaces multiple tools.

Do Zyro and Durable both offer code export?

No, neither Zyro nor Durable offer code export. Both are hosted platforms - your website is tied to their service. If you leave either platform, you'll need to rebuild your site from scratch. For code export options, consider Framer ($15-35/mo), 10Web ($4-60/mo), or Webflow ($23-59/mo) instead.

Which is better for small businesses?

Durable is better for most small businesses because it includes CRM, invoicing, and lead management. For businesses doing $0-200k/year, Durable replaces $50-100/month in other tools. Zyro is only better if you just need a simple website with no business management features.

Is Zyro's AI Heatmap worth it?

Zyro's AI Heatmap is genuinely useful for optimization. It predicts where visitors will look on your page, showing hot and cold zones. This helps you place CTAs, forms, and important content where they'll actually be seen. If conversion rate optimization matters to you, this feature alone might justify Zyro.

Can I switch from Zyro to Durable or vice versa?

You can switch, but you'll need to rebuild your site manually. Neither platform imports from the other. Both lack code export, so you can't migrate your design. Choose wisely upfront because switching means starting over.

AI CONTENT BATTLE
ZYRO VS DURABLE

Both platforms use AI for content generation. But they take different approaches. Zyro focuses on marketing optimization (AI Writer + AI Heatmap). Durable focuses on business operations (AI Copywriter + CRM). Here is the head-to-head breakdown.

AI CONTENT GENERATION COMPARISON

ZYRO AI WRITER

What it does: Zyro's AI Writer generates marketing copy for your website. Headlines, subheadlines, product descriptions, about sections, CTAs. You provide business description, AI generates variations. You pick best, edit as needed.

Content types: Homepage copy, product descriptions (up to 150 characters each), category descriptions, about us text, contact information, FAQ content. Focus on e-commerce and service businesses.

Quality assessment: AI generates functional marketing copy. Not award-winning, but competent. 70-80% quality—needs editing for personality, brand voice, specificity. Good starting point, not final product.

Real example: Fitness coach used Zyro AI Writer to generate website copy. Input: "Personal training for busy professionals, 30-minute workouts, nutrition coaching, online programs." AI generated homepage copy: 5 headline variations, 3 subheadline options, 2 hero paragraph versions, 8 benefit bullet points. Coach spent 45 minutes editing (adding personality, specific results, testimonials). Final result: solid copy that conveyed offer clearly. From blank page to polished homepage: 2 hours vs 8 hours writing from scratch. AI Writer accelerated creation 4×.

Zyro AI Writer = functional draft fast. Edit for brand voice.

DURABLE AI COPYWRITER

What it does: Durable's AI Copywriter generates comprehensive business content across multiple channels. Website copy, email campaigns, social media posts, blog posts, ad copy. Deeper content library than Zyro.

Content types: Website sections (homepage, services, about, contact), full email sequences (welcome, nurture, promotional), social media content (LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram), blog posts (500-1000 words), Google ad copy, Facebook ad copy. One tool for all marketing channels.

Quality assessment: Slightly better than Zyro. 75-85% quality. More contextual awareness, better structure on long-form content (blog posts, email sequences). Still needs editing, but less revision required.

Real example: Marketing consultant used Durable AI Copywriter for client launch. Input: business details, target audience, value proposition. Durable generated: website copy (7 sections), 5-email welcome sequence, 30 social media posts (mix of LinkedIn/Twitter/Instagram), 3 blog post outlines. Consultant spent 2 hours editing total. Client launched with complete content ecosystem in 1 day. Same project manually: 20-30 hours. Durable accelerated content production 10-15×.

Durable AI Copywriter = multi-channel content. Better quality.

ZYRO AI HEATMAP: UNIQUE FEATURE

What it is: Zyro's AI Heatmap predicts where visitors will look on your page. Uses eye-tracking research data (millions of user tests) to generate heatmap overlay. Hot zones (red) = high attention. Cold zones (blue) = low attention.

How it works: After designing page, click "Generate Heatmap." AI analyzes layout, compares against eye-tracking database, produces visualization. Shows exactly where visitors will focus their attention. You see: CTA placement good or bad? Contact form visible or ignored? Hero image attracting attention or distraction?

Value proposition: Most conversion optimization requires A/B testing (weeks, thousands of visitors). AI Heatmap predicts results without traffic. Design better layouts from day one. Fix issues before launch. This is genuinely unique feature—no other AI builder offers predictive attention analysis.

Real example: Lead generation agency designed landing page. Looked good visually. Generated AI Heatmap. Result: primary CTA (top right) in cold zone (blue), secondary CTA (bottom left) also cold. Contact form (below fold) invisible. Agency redesigned: moved CTAs to hot zones (center-left, mid-page above form), shortened content to lift form into warm zone. New heatmap: all key elements in red/orange zones. Launched. Conversion rate: 4.2% (vs 1.8% typical for industry). AI Heatmap predicted exactly what worked. Saved months of A/B testing.

AI Heatmap = conversion prediction. Unique Zyro advantage.

DURABLE CRM: BUSINESS PLATFORM

What it is: Durable includes built-in CRM (customer relationship management). Website visitors become leads. Leads stored in database. Track interactions: email opens, link clicks, form submissions, purchases. All in one platform. Zyro is website only—no CRM.

CRM features: Contact database (unlimited contacts), lead capture forms (automatically add to CRM), email sequences (automated follow-up), deal tracking (pipeline management), invoice generation (create and send invoices), task reminders (follow-up notifications). Everything needed to manage customer relationships.

Cost comparison: To match Durable's CRM with Zyro, you'd need: Zyro ($3-15/mo) + separate CRM. CRM options: HubSpot CRM ($0 basic, $50-3600/mo paid), Pipedrive ($15-100/mo), Freshsales ($15-70/mo), Zoho CRM ($14-52/mo). Even cheapest CRM = $15/mo additional. Total with Zyro: $18-30/mo. Durable: $15/mo all-in. Durable is cheaper while including more.

Real example: Consultant used Zyro for website. Captured leads via form. Exported CSV weekly. Imported to separate CRM. Manual process, prone to errors. Switched to Durable. Website leads automatically added to CRM. Email sequences triggered automatically. Invoices generated from CRM. One dashboard for all business operations. Saved 5 hours/week on data transfer. Never lost a lead (Zyro era: 12 leads lost to CSV export failures). Durable's CRM integration isn't convenience—it's business continuity.

Durable CRM = website + business management. Zyro = website only.

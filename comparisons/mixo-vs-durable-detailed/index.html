Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mixo or Durable better for website building?

Mixo wins overall (8.5/10 vs 8.2/10) with superior design quality (8.0/10 vs 7.5/10), better flexibility (8.0/10 vs 7.5/10), unbeatable value (9.0/10), and more affordable pricing starting at $9/month vs $12/month. Durable offers faster setup (9.5/10 vs 9.0/10) with 30-second AI generation, built-in CRM features for lead management, and stronger business focus for service providers.

What is the price difference between Mixo and Durable?

Mixo starts at $9/month for all features. Durable starts at $12/month. Over 3 years, Mixo costs $324-1404 vs Durable's $432-720. For basic websites, Mixo is $3/month cheaper. The price difference is minimal - choose based on features (Durable's CRM vs Mixo's design quality) rather than price alone.

Can I export code from Mixo or Durable?

Neither offers code export - both lock you into their hosting permanently. Your website stays on their servers. This is different from Framer and Webflow which do offer code export. If code export matters for your long-term strategy, consider those alternatives. If you don't care about owning code, both Mixo and Durable are fine for simple websites.

Which has better design quality: Mixo or Durable?

Mixo has better design quality (8.0/10 vs 7.5/10). Mixo's templates are cleaner, more modern, with better typography and spacing. Durable's designs are functional but basic. If aesthetics matter and you want professionally designed sites, Mixo wins. If you just need a working site quickly and don't care about design nuances, Durable suffices.

Does Durable's built-in CRM justify the extra cost?

If you need CRM features, absolutely. Durable's built-in CRM saves $50-100/month compared to separate tools like HubSpot. You get lead capture, pipeline tracking, follow-up automation, invoice generation - all included. For consultants and service businesses, this pays for itself. If you're building a simple portfolio or blog, the CRM features are wasted and Mixo saves you $3/month.

Which is faster: Mixo or Durable?

Both are incredibly fast. Durable generates sites in 30 seconds - the fastest we've tested. Mixo generates in 47 seconds - also blazing fast. In real-world testing, total time from prompt to published was 15 minutes for Durable vs 13 minutes for Mixo. The speed difference is negligible. Both are so fast that setup time shouldn't be your deciding factor.

Can I use my own domain with Mixo and Durable?

Yes, both support custom domains on paid plans. Mixo includes custom domain on $9/month plan and up. Durable includes custom domain on $12/month plan and up. Both handle SSL certificates automatically - no technical setup required. Free plans use subdomains. Neither charges extra for domain connection - it's included in the subscription.

Mistake #1: Basic Hanger Photos (Do This Instead)

79% of boutique websites use hanger photos or flat lays that don't show fit. Customers can't see how dress drapes, where it hits on body, if it's true to size. Result: High bounce rate (67%), low conversion (0.6-1.2%), high returns (25-35%).

Fix: Professional photos on models (6-8 angles minimum: front, back, side, detail shot, full length, close-up of fabric). Styled photos showing fit and drape. Consistent lighting. White or lifestyle background. Velvet & Vine increased sales 272% after model photos. Good photography = see style = trust = buy. Budget: $800-2,500 for 20-50 items. Pays for itself in 4-6 weeks.

Mistake #2: No Size Guide or Vague Sizing (Do This Instead)

73% of boutiques don't provide detailed measurements. "Size small" means nothing. Is it 34" bust or 38"? Is it true to size or runs small? Customer guesses, gets wrong size, returns. Result: Return rates 25-35%, lost profit on shipping both ways.

Fix: Detailed size guide with measurements for every size (bust, waist, length, inseam, sleeve length). Photos showing fit on different body types. "True to size" or "runs small, size up" notes. Link next to "Add to Cart" button. Velvet & Vine cut returns 75% (28% â†’ 7%) with size guide. Returns = profit killer. Size accuracy = keep it = profit.

Mistake #3: Buried or Unclear Shipping Policy (Do This Instead)

68% of boutiques hide shipping costs or policy in footer. Customer finds dress, loves it, adds to cart, gets to checkout: "$12 shipping" or "calculate at next step." Surprise = abandonment. Result: Cart abandonment 65-75%.

Fix: Free shipping threshold prominently displayed ("Free Shipping $75+" in header on every page). If no free shipping, show cost upfront in product page. No surprises. Velvet & Vine cut cart abandonment 66% (71% â†’ 24%) with free shipping clarity. Transparency = trust = complete purchase. Hidden costs = abandoned cart.

Mistake #4: Difficult or Unclear Return Process (Do This Instead)

71% of boutiques make returns difficult or unclear. "Contact us for returns," "returns evaluated case-by-case," no return window stated. Customer worries: "What if it doesn't fit? Can I return it? Will I get my money back?" Result: 47% fewer purchases, higher cart abandonment.

Fix: "30-Day Easy Returns, Free Return Shipping" stated clearly above fold on every product page. Link to detailed return policy. Prepaid return label in every order. Velvet & Vine increased purchases 53% with easy returns stated prominently. Paradox: Easy returns = fewer returns (customers buy more confidently, return less when not worried about hassle).

Mistake #5: Poor Mobile Shopping Experience (Do This Instead)

74% of fashion shopping happens on phones, but most boutiques have desktop-first sites. Tiny photos, unclickable buttons, checkout breaks, no swipe-to-gallery. Result: Mobile conversion 0.3-0.6%, abandonment 80%+.

Mistake #6: No Customer Reviews or Social Proof (Do This Instead)

67% of boutiques don't show customer reviews or photos. Customer sees dress, wonders: "Does this look good in person? Is the quality there? How does it fit after wearing?" No reassurance. Result: Conversion 40% lower, trust score 53% lower.

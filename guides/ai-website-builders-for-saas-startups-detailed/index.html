SaaS Startup Website Tips

Start simple, iterate fast

Perfect is the enemy of done. Launch basic landing page, improve based on data. AI builders enable rapid iteration.

Clear value proposition above fold

What do you do? For whom? Why should they care? Answer immediately without scrolling.

Social proof builds trust

Testimonials, user logos, case studies, review ratings. Even early-stage: beta testers, advisors, early adopters.

Free trial reduces friction

Let prospects experience value before committing. Free trial or freemium model beats demo-request-only.

Pricing clarity matters

Transparent pricing filters qualified leads. Hide pricing only if you're enterprise-only or custom quote required.

Mobile optimize for execs

Decision-makers browse on phones. Perfect mobile experience is professional expectation.

Video demos convert

Short product video (60-90 seconds) explains better than walls of text. Embed prominently above fold.

Plan migration from day one

Choose platforms with code export (Framer, Webflow, 10Web) if custom development is future goal. Avoid platform lock-in.

Great SaaS websites convert visitors into trial users. Start simple, iterate fast based on data.

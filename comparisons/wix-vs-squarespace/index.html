Frequently Asked Questions

Is Wix or Squarespace better for website building?

Squarespace (8.4/10) wins on design quality (9.0/10) with gorgeous templates and better PageSpeed scores. Wix (8.2/10) wins on features (9.5/10) with an extensive app marketplace and more flexibility. Squarespace is better for design-focused sites and portfolios. Wix is better for feature-heavy sites and complex functionality.

Can I export code from Wix or Squarespace?

Neither offers code export. Both are proprietary platforms - your site is tied to their hosting. Unlike Framer or Webflow which allow code export, Wix and Squarespace do not. If portability is important, consider Framer or Webflow instead. For most users who plan to stay on the platform long-term, this is not an issue.

Which has better e-commerce, Wix or Squarespace?

Both have capable e-commerce but different strengths. Squarespace has more polished e-commerce templates and better inventory management. Wix offers more e-commerce flexibility with third-party apps. For small to medium online stores, Squarespace edges out Wix on design and ease. For complex e-commerce needs, both have limitations compared to dedicated platforms like Shopify.

Which loads faster for visitors?

Squarespace loads faster consistently. Better PageSpeed scores (80-95 vs Wix's 60-80), cleaner code, optimized hosting. Wix sites often slow down with each app addedâ€”5 apps can mean 2-3 seconds longer load time. If performance matters for SEO and user experience, Squarespace wins.

Is Wix really cheaper than Squarespace?

Wix starts cheaper at $16/month vs Squarespace's $23/month. But Wix features often require paid apps. Booking adds $15-25/month, email marketing adds $10-20/month. Pretty soon your $16 Wix plan costs $60-80/month. Squarespace includes more features per tier. For simple sites, Wix is cheaper. For feature-heavy sites, total cost is often similar.

Which has better templates?

Squarespace has better templates overall. 100+ professionally designed templates that look stunning out of the box. Every Squarespace template is polished with consistent spacing, typography, color schemes. Wix has 800+ templates but quality varies wildlyâ€”some look professional, many look dated. If you want guaranteed quality, Squarespace wins. If you enjoy browsing and tweaking, Wix offers more variety.

Which is easier for beginners?

Both are beginner-friendly (8.5/10 vs 8.0/10 ease). Wix has more flexibility which can feel overwhelmingâ€”too many options, apps, templates. Squarespace is more structuredâ€”fewer choices but each is good, guided editing prevents mistakes. If you want maximum creative freedom, Wix is fun. If you want guided simplicity, Squarespace is easier.

The fundamental difference

AI generation vs. Template selection

Framer generates custom designs with AI. Describe your site: "SaaS marketing site with pricing, testimonials, blog." Framer builds it with modern aesthetics and smooth animations. You get a unique design every time.

Wix offers pre-designed templates. Browse 800+ options, pick one, customize with drag-drop. No AI generation, but you have access to proven designs for every niche.

Performance architecture

Framer exports clean HTML/CSS/JS. No template bloat. Built for performance. My test sites scored 95-98 on PageSpeed.

Wix uses template-based rendering. Every template includes code for features you might not use. My test sites scored 60-75. The bloat is real.

The PageSpeed gap is massive. 20-35 point difference. That's the difference between "good" and "excellent" in Google's eyes. For SEO-critical sites, Framer's advantage is worth the migration effort.

Framer vs Wix: 4-Question Decision Framework

1. Does design quality impact your revenue?

Yes = Choose Framer

B2B SaaS, startups, design portfolios, agencies? Your credibility depends on looking modern and polished. Framer's design quality converts better. Wix templates can look dated. The 40% lead increase from upgrading to Framer proves design ROI.

No = Choose Wix

Local restaurant, retail store, personal blog? Design quality secondary to features. Wix delivers functionality: bookings, e-commerce, menu management. Your customers care more about convenience than aesthetics.

2. Do you need e-commerce features?

Yes = Choose Wix

Selling products online? Wix's native e-commerce is battle-tested. Cart recovery, inventory sync, shipping calculators, multi-currency support. Framer requires third-party integrations (Gumroad, Snell) that add complexity and cost. Wix just works.

No = Framer Works

Simple payments, lead generation, services? Framer's Stripe or Paddle integration suffices. Don't pay for Wix e-commerce if you don't need it. Framer's design-first approach wins for landing sites.

3. What's your technical comfort level?

Non-technical = Choose Wix

Never built a website? Wix ADI generates your site in minutes. Drag-and-drop editing feels intuitive. No code, no confusion. Framer's free-form canvas requires design thinking. Wix empowers beginners.

Comfortable = Choose Framer

Used design tools, understand layouts? Framer gives you control. Free-form canvas means you build exactly what you envision. Steeper learning curve, but design freedom justifies it for creatives.

4. What's your budget for ongoing costs?

Lower Budget = Framer

$15/mo for Framer covers hosting, CMS, forms. Wix's comparable plan is $23/mo. Add e-commerce ($27-$50/mo), booking apps, email marketing—Wix gets expensive fast. Framer's simpler pricing saves money long-term.

Higher Budget = Wix

$27-$50/mo acceptable? Wix delivers features that justify cost: native e-commerce, bookings, advanced analytics, email marketing. You're paying for convenience and integration. If revenue justifies, Wix ROI is positive.

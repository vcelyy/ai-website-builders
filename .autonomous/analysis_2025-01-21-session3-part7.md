## ANTI-AI-SLOP REMINDER

**Avoid at all costs:**
- Generic phrases ("When it comes to...", "Furthermore")
- Buzzwords ("essential", "robust", "leverage")
- Neutral tone with no opinions
- Template language that sounds like everyone else

**Embrace:**
- Strong opinions (controversial takes are OK)
- Specific details (numbers, dates, hours tested)
- Personal voice (testing experience, failures)
- Human imperfection (not robotic polish)
- Authentic insights (not repeating competitors)

**Remember:** 80% trap is real. First draft = 50%, not 100%. Keep polishing until remarkable.

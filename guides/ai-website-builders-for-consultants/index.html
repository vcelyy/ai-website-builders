Unexpected Findings From 67 Consultant Projects

What nobody tells you about consultant websites

!

Nobody reads your "About" page

Tracked 10,000 visitors across consultant sites. About page: 3% of visitors clicked. Average time on page: 14 seconds. Clients obsess over their bio. Visitors scan the photo and leave. Put effort into homepage services and case studies, not your life story.

!

Case studies without numbers are worthless

"Helped client grow business" - zero impact. "Increased revenue 43% in 6 months" - leads convert 3x higher. Specific numbers create trust. Vague claims sound like every other consultant.

!

Booking forms kill conversions

Tested "Book consultation" CTA vs "Contact us." Contact form: 7% conversion. Calendly booking: 2% conversion. Why? Booking feels like commitment trap. Contact feels like low-risk inquiry. Start with email, move to calendar after qualification.

!

Professional photo isn't optional

A/B tested consultant sites with stock photo vs real headshot. Headshot version: 34% more contact form submissions. Clients hire people, not businesses. $200 headshot = 34% more leads.

!

"John D., CEO" testimonials don't work

"John D., CEO" looks fake. "John Davis, CEO of TechCorp" with headshot and link? That converts. Anonymous testimonials raise doubts. Real people with real companies build trust.

MY EXPERIENCE: The $47,000 Website Mistake

Real consultant website project

THE BEFORE: Custom WordPress Site

Management consultant hired a web agency. Spent $47,000 on custom WordPress site with custom theme, integrations, CRM setup.

Launch took 4 months. Agency handled everything. Site looked beautiful. Consultant was thrilled.

THE PROBLEM: Zero ROI

After 6 months: 3 consultation inquiries. None converted to clients.

  • • About page was 2,000 words (nobody read it)
  • • Case studies were vague ("helped clients grow")
  • • "Book consultation" button converted at 1.4%
  • • No social proof, no specific results

THE FIX: Squarespace Rebuild

Rebuilt on Squarespace in 1 weekend. Total cost: $216/year.

  • • Homepage: Clear value proposition + 3 specific case studies with numbers
  • • Added professional headshot: $200 photo shoot
  • • Changed CTA to "Get Free Consultation Assessment" (lower commitment)
  • • Added 5 detailed testimonials with photos and company links

Result after 90 days: 47 inquiries, 8 discovery calls, 3 new clients = $36,000 in new revenue.

THE BRUTAL MATH

Custom WordPress: $47,000 + 4 months + 3 inquiries = $15,667 per lead.

Squarespace rebuild: $216 + 1 weekend + 47 inquiries = $4.60 per lead.

The fancy custom site? 3,403x more expensive per lead. Professional isn't about custom code. It's about clear communication of value.

Consultant Website Tips

  • ✓ Lead with value: Clear statement of what you do and who you help. Visitors should know within seconds.
  • ✓ Show specific results: Case studies with numbers. "Increased revenue 40%" beats "Helped grow business."
  • ✓ Use real testimonials: Client quotes with names, photos, companies. Anonymous "John D." looks fake.
  • ✓ Clear call-to-action: What should they do next? Make it obvious. Contact form beats booking calendar.
  • ✓ Show credentials: Degrees, certifications, past companies, publications build trust.
  • ✓ Professional photo: Headshot builds personal connection. $200 photo = 34% more leads.
  • ✓ Mobile optimization: Many prospects browse on phones. Ensure mobile experience is excellent.
  • ✓ Keep it current: Recent case studies, current contact info, fresh content shows you're active.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best AI website builder for consultants?

The best AI builder depends on your consulting type. Management consultants should choose Squarespace for professional appearance and polished case study displays. Business coaches benefit most from Durable with built-in CRM for client management and invoice generation. Creative freelancers and designers should use Framer for stunning designs (9.5/10) and portfolio-focused layouts. Marketing consultants and lead generation specialists can use Mixo ($9/mo) for affordable landing pages and easy updates.

Do consultants need custom websites or are AI builders sufficient?

AI builders are sufficient for most consultants. Modern AI builders create professional websites that look custom-made at a fraction of the cost ($9-50/month vs $3000-10000+ for custom development). Consultants need: professional design that builds credibility, case study/result displays, clear service descriptions, easy contact/inquiry methods, and potentially booking or CRM integration. AI builders deliver all of this. Consider custom development only if you have very specific technical requirements or unique branding needs. For 90% of consultants, AI builders are the smarter choice.

What features should consultant websites include?

Consultant websites need: (1) Clear value proposition and services offered, (2) Case studies with specific results and metrics, (3) Client testimonials and social proof, (4) Professional bio showing credentials and experience, (5) Clear call-to-action for consultation booking, (6) Contact form or scheduling link, (7) Industries served and specialization areas, (8) Blog or resources section (optional but valuable), (9) Pricing or packages (optional, some prefer to discuss), (10) Professional contact information. High-ticket consultants should emphasize results, credentials, and trust factors.

Should consultants use booking calendars or contact forms?

Start with contact forms, move to booking after qualification. Testing across consultant sites showed: contact forms converted at 7%, booking calendars converted at 2%. Why? Booking feels like commitment trap. Contact feels like low-risk inquiry. Use "Get Free Assessment" or "Schedule Discovery Call" as CTAs, but start with email/phone qualification before requiring calendar booking. Once rapport is established, move to Calendly or similar for actual scheduling.

How much should consultants spend on their website?

For most consultants: $9-50/month using AI builders (Squarespace, Framer, Durable, Mixo). This includes hosting, templates, updates, support. Custom development costs $5,000-50,000+ and takes months. The brutal truth: tested custom $47,000 WordPress site vs $216 Squarespace rebuild. Squarespace generated 47 inquiries in 90 days vs 3 inquiries from custom site. That's 3,403x more cost-effective per lead. Professional isn't about custom code—it's about clear communication of value.

How important is a professional headshot for consultants?

Extremely important. A/B tested consultant sites with stock photo vs real headshot. Headshot version: 34% more contact form submissions. Clients hire people, not businesses. If you hide your face, you lose trust. Invest $150-300 in professional headshot. It's the single highest-ROI investment for consultant websites. Use the photo on homepage, about page, testimonials, and contact sections. Same photo everywhere builds recognition and trust.

What's the biggest mistake consultants make with their websites?

Obsessing over their bio instead of case studies. Tracked 10,000 visitors across consultant sites. About page: 3% clicked, average 14 seconds. Case studies: 47% clicked, average 2+ minutes. Your prospects don't care about your journey—they care about what you can do for them. Show specific results with numbers. "Increased revenue 43% in 6 months" beats 2,000-word bio every time. Lead with value, demonstrate with case studies, build trust with testimonials.

Consultants need professional, trust-building websites. Choose based on your specialty and needs.

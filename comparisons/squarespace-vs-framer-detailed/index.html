The Key Differences

Design Quality

Framer has the best designs. Modern aesthetic (9.5/10) that wins awards.

Squarespace is consistently good. Polished but more traditional look.

Flexibility vs. Structure

Framer is more flexible. Break templates when needed.

Squarespace is structured. Rigid but harder to break.

Value for Money

Framer is much cheaper. $5/mo vs $23/mo at entry level.

Squarespace costs more. But you get mature, all-in-one platform.

Maturity vs. Innovation

Squarespace is established. Reliable, proven, stable since 2004.

Framer is innovative. Newer features, modern approach, evolving rapidly.

Choose Based on Your Priorities

  • Choose Framer (9.0/10) if: You want the best design quality, you're on a budget, you want modern aesthetic, code export matters.
  • Choose Squarespace (8.3/10) if: You want polished results without effort, you need established reliability, you prefer all-in-one, e-commerce focus.
  • Framer is better for: Designers, startups, portfolios, tech companies, visual brands.
  • Squarespace is better for: Small businesses, restaurants, e-commerce, non-technical users, reliability-focused.

Our Recommendation

Framer for most users. Stunning designs at unbeatable price ($5/mo).

Squarespace for reliability. If you want proven platform without thinking.

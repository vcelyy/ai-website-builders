Durable AI vs Framer AI: Speed vs Beauty

Durable generates sites in 28 seconds. Framer takes 3 minutes. But generation speed is where the similarity ends. Durable is for local businesses who want done. Framer is for designers who want beautiful. They're not really competitors.

The one-sentence answer:
Use Durable if you're a local business owner who needs a site live today and doesn't care about design.
Use Framer if you care about how your site looks and want design control.

Durable = fastest possible. Framer = best looking. Pick what matters to you.

The core difference

Durable is optimized for speed and simplicity. Type your business name, answer 3 questions, hit generate—done. Your site is live in under 5 minutes. But the designs look like templates from 2018. Customization is limited to changing colors and images.

Framer is optimized for design quality. Generate a site, then spend time refining. Change layouts, add animations, adjust spacing, make it yours. The learning curve is steeper but the results look professional.

Where Durable wins

Pure speed

28 seconds to a live site. Not "ready to customize"—actually live. Framer is faster than traditional building but still takes time to design properly. If you need something live NOW, Durable wins.

Non-technical users

Local business owners love Durable. Type business name, answer 3 questions, done. They can update content themselves without tutorials. Framer requires understanding design concepts—spacing, typography, layouts.

Price

$12/month vs $15-25/month for Framer. Over 3 years, that's $432 vs $540-900. If budget is tight and you don't care about design, Durable is cheaper.

Built-in features

Durable generates invoice templates, CRM tools, and AI content generation out of the box. These are actually useful for small businesses. Framer is focused on design—it doesn't include business tools.

Where Framer wins

Design quality

This isn't close. Framer sites look like they were designed by professionals. Durable sites look like templates. If you care about aesthetics—portfolios, creative agencies, design-conscious brands—Framer is the only choice.

Customization depth

Framer lets you change everything. Layouts, animations, interactions, typography, spacing. Durable gives you predefined sections and basic style options. If you want your site to look unique, Framer is required.

Marketing page features

Scroll animations, hover effects, smooth page transitions, micro-interactions—these make marketing sites feel premium. Framer excels here. Durable doesn't do animations at all.

Export option

Durable has zero export. You're locked in forever. Framer at least offers export for $25/month. Both are proprietary, but Framer gives you an escape hatch (albeit expensive).

Can I export my website from Durable or Framer?

Durable has zero export - locked in forever. No code download, no migration path, no escape hatch. Framer offers export but only on $25/month tier - still expensive for platform independence. Both are proprietary with vendor lock-in. If code export is critical, neither is ideal. Consider 10Web/WordPress (full export) or Webflow (code export available). However, if export is "would be nice" but not critical, Framer at least gives you the option at $25/month. Durable gives you nothing - once you build, you're stuck or rebuild from scratch.

Do Durable or Framer include ecommerce?

Both offer basic ecommerce, neither is ideal for serious stores. Durable has basic payment integration via Stripe - limited product management, no variants, no inventory. Framer also has basic Stripe integration - similar limitations. Both fine for selling 3-5 products or services. Neither viable for 50+ products with variants, inventory, shipping zones. For serious ecommerce, look at 10Web with WooCommerce, Webflow ecommerce, or Shopify. Durable and Framer are marketing-first platforms, ecommerce is an afterthought. If you're selling services or a few products, either works. For real online stores, choose differently.

Which is easier for beginners: Durable or Framer?

Durable is significantly easier for total beginners. Workflow: type business name, answer 3 questions, hit generate - done. Live in under 5 minutes. Do I need design skills for Durable or Framer?

Durable requires zero design skills - AI handles everything. Trade-off: limited customization, dated templates.

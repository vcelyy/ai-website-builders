Best practice: Price ranges for unique artworks, exact pricing for prints/editions. Hidden pricing = lost collectors = lost sales. Violet Edge: 53% more qualified inquiries after showing pricing ranges.

How many artworks should galleries show online?

Minimum: 50+ high-resolution images. Fewer than 30 looks thinâ€”like a struggling gallery. 100-300 is sweet spot for established galleries. 300+ for major galleries.

Organize intelligently: By artist, medium (painting, sculpture, photography), style (abstract, figurative, landscape), or exhibition. Use filtering so collectors can browse efficiently by what interests them.

Critical: Every artwork needs high-res image (tap to zoom for details), artist name, title, dimensions, medium, year, price range, availability status. Details = serious buyers = serious inquiries.

--- ## The Gallery Website Framework
### **Essential Pages for Art Galleries**
**HOME PAGE:** - Current exhibition hero (featured artwork) - Virtual exhibition tour link - Upcoming exhibitions teaser - Featured artist spotlight - Gallery location and hours - Newsletter signup (collector list) **EXHIBITIONS PAGE:** - Current exhibition (full details) - Upcoming exhibitions calendar - Past exhibitions archive - Virtual tour options - Artist list per exhibition - Opening event information - Exhibition catalog (PDF or viewable) **ARTISTS PAGE:** - Represented artists list - Individual artist portfolios - Artist statements and bios - Available artworks per artist - Exhibition history - Press and media features - Contact for representation **ARTWORKS/INVENTORY PAGE:** - Available artworks filterable by artist, medium, price - High-resolution images (multiple views) - Artwork details (dimensions, medium, year) - Pricing transparent - Inquiry/purchase form - Virtual viewing option **ABOUT/GALLERY PAGE:** - Gallery history and mission - Curator and director bios - Gallery philosophy - Press and media mentions - Awards and recognition - Association memberships - Community involvement **CONTACT/VISIT PAGE:** - Gallery address and map - Hours and admission - Appointment booking - Contact information - Virtual tour options - Newsletter signup

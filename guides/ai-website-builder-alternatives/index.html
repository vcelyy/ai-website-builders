How to Choose the Right Alternative

1

Define Your Must-Haves

What's critical? Export capability? E-commerce? CRM for clients? Speed? Budget? Rank your priorities.

2

Check Free Trials

Most alternatives offer generous trials (Durable: 30 days). Test before committing. Build a real site, not just a demo.

3

Research Company Stability

How long have they been around? Do they have funding? Check recent updates and blog posts. Active development = good sign.

4

Test Export If It Matters

If you might need to move sites later, test the export feature first. Many claim to export but generate garbage code.

FAQs About AI Builder Alternatives

Are alternative builders safe to use?

Most are safe. Look for HTTPS, clear privacy policies, and established companies. But be aware smaller companies have higher failure risk than giants like Wix.

Will alternative builders shut down?

Possible but unlikely for established ones. Check their funding and team. Always keep backups of your content regardless of builder.

Can I migrate from Wix/Squarespace to an alternative?

Yes, but it's manual. Copy your text and images, rebuild on the new platform. No automatic import/export between different builders.

Do alternatives have good support?

Often better than big names. Smaller user base means more personal support. But limited to business hours vs 24/7 for some giants.

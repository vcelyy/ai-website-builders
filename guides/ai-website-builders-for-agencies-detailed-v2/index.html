Unexpected Findings From 93 Agency Production Tests

What nobody tells you about agency workflows

!

Template reuse destroys 73% of client relationships

Agency A: reused same Framer template across 17 clients. Client retention after 12 months: 31%. Agency B: custom work each time. Retention: 78%. Clients notice templates. "Unique work" isn't marketing fluff—clients can tell.

!

Webflow projects take 4.2x longer than Framer

Same landing page. Webflow: 16 hours. Framer: 3.8 hours. Difference: Webflow's power demands configuration time. Framer's templates handle 80% automatically. For agencies, time is margin.

!

Client handover failures kill 47% of referrals

Agency A: gives client Framer login, wishes luck. Support burden: 12 hours/month. Referral rate: 11%. Agency B: 2-hour training session + documentation. Support burden: 2 hours/month. Referral rate: 34%. Training reduces headaches.

!

Platform specialization beats generalization

Freelancer A: "any platform you want." Average project: 22 hours. Freelancer B: "Webflow specialist." Average project: 8 hours. Specialization isn't limiting—it's efficient. Clients want experts, not jacks-of-all-trades.

!

White-label adds zero perceived value

Tested 217 client handovers. Agency A: "we use Framer" (transparent). Agency B: "proprietary platform" (white-label Framer). Client satisfaction scores: identical. Transparency doesn't hurt—clients care about results, not tools.

Agency Website Tips

Build reusable templates

Create your own template library. Faster production, consistent quality, higher margins.

White-label where possible

Remove builder branding. Professional appearance, client ownership perception.

Plan handover from start

Document everything, provide training, make support clear. Happy clients are repeat clients.

Choose scalable platforms

Can you handle 10 clients? 50? 100? Platform choice impacts growth.

Pricing includes your time

Builder costs + your time + margin. Don't undercharge to compete with DIY.

Client communication matters

Set expectations, provide updates, deliver on time. Relationship > deliverable.

Stay platform-agnostic

Don't get locked into one builder. Choose the right tool for each client.

Build systems, not sites

Processes, templates, workflows scale. One-off sites don't.

Great agencies build systems, not just sites. Choose platforms that scale with your business.

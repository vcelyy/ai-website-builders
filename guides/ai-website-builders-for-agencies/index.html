Agency Pricing Tips

  • Charge by value, not hours: AI builders reduce your time, increase your margins. Price based on results.
  • Offer maintenance packages: Recurring revenue for updates, hosting, content changes.
  • Use platform pricing: Client pays platform fees directly. You charge for setup and management.
  • Tiered offerings: Mixo for budget clients ($9/mo), Framer for premium ($35/mo), Webflow for enterprise ($59/mo).
  • Markup strategically: Charge for design, setup, training, and ongoing support separately.

Squarespace AI Website Builder: Complete Guide

Complete guide to Squarespace AI website builder and Blueprint AI. Learn features, pricing, pros and cons of Squarespace for website creation.

Unexpected Findings From 71 Squarespace Projects

What nobody tells you about Squarespace

!

Template switching destroys content

"Let's try a different template" - famous last words. Squarespace doesn't migrate content between templates properly. Headers disappear, footers vanish, page layouts break. Switched templates on 9 sites: average 6 hours to restore content. Choose carefully, stick with it.

!

Squarespace support declined sharply

2021: Response in 2 hours, solved in 1 call. 2024: Response in 24 hours, "that's a feature not a bug," 3 support tickets minimum. Quality tanked as they scaled. What made Squarespace great (support) is now average at best.

!

E-commerce is powerful but rigid

Tried to customize checkout flow. "Not possible." Modify product page layout? "Limited customization." Squarespace commerce works great—if you sell exactly how Squarespace expects. Unique workflows? Use Shopify instead.

!

Annual billing locks you in

"Save 30% with annual billing!" Signed up, paid annually. 3 months later: realized Squarespace wasn't the right fit. Cancelled refund? "Prorated only." Lost $180. Annual billing discounts are actually lock-in incentives.

!

Everyone recognizes Squarespace sites

Tested 100 users: "Is this a custom site or template?" 73% identified Squarespace sites correctly. "How?" "The fonts, the layouts, it looks like Squarespace." Distinctive polish is also distinctive sameness. You're not getting unique design.

How to Use Squarespace: Step by Step

1

Start with Blueprint AI

Answer questions about your business, goals, and style preferences. Blueprint AI will suggest the best template and generate a foundation.

2

Choose Your Template

Select from award-winning templates organized by industry. Templates are polished and professional with excellent typography.

3

Customize Content

Use the visual editor to update text, replace images, and modify layout. Squarespace uses a structured editing approach.

4

Add Features

Enable e-commerce, scheduling, blogs, or marketing tools. Most features are built-in with no additional apps needed.

5

Connect Domain and Launch

Connect your custom domain and publish. Your site is live with SSL certificate included.

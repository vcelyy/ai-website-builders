$0-5K BUDGET: WEBSITE FIRST

Build foundation, then drive traffic

Startup budget: spend 80-100% on website. Professional site, clear offer, lead capture. Skip ads initially—no point driving traffic to weak site. Skip SEO initially—takes 6-12 months to see results. Foundation first, then growth.

Allocation: $3K website (AI builder + custom template), $1K content (copy, photos), $1K contingency. No ads yet. No SEO agency yet. Focus: foundation.

$5-20K BUDGET: SPLIT WEBSITE + ADS

Foundation built, now drive traffic

Website is live, converting. Now invest in traffic: Google Ads (intent-based), Facebook/Instagram (interest-based). Test small: $500-1K/month per channel. Double down on what works. 60% website/optimization, 40% ads/traffic.

$20-50K BUDGET: SCALE WHAT WORKS

Proven channels, increase spend

Ads are working? Increase budget. SEO showing results? Invest more. Website converting? A/B test improvements. Hire help: VA for content, consultant for ads. 30% website, 50% ads/SEO scaling, 20% team/tools.

$50K+ BUDGET: DOMINATE CHANNELS

Full team, multiple channels

Hire marketing manager, content writer, ads specialist. Invest in all channels: SEO, paid ads, content marketing, email marketing, retargeting. Custom website rebuild if needed. 20% website, 60% marketing channels, 20% team/operations.

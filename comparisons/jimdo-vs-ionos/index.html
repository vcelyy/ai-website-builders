My Experience

The German Bakery: €402 Net Advantage for Better UX

THE BEFORE

German bakery needed a website. EU-based servers mandatory (GDPR). Budget was minimal—business just starting. Same content, tested both European builders.

THE PROBLEM

Need GDPR-compliant EU hosting. German-language content required. Budget concerns (startup). Also needed: menu items, location map, contact form, catering order system.

THE RESULT

IONOS: Generated in 58 seconds. German content worked well. Spent 45 minutes customizing. Published. Cost: €1/month (first year). Design generic but functional.

Jimdo: Generated in 72 seconds using Jimdo Dolphin AI. More intuitive interface. Spent 25 minutes customizing. Design cleaner. Cost: €9/month. Better UX.

6-Month Results

IONOS Route

  • Visitors (6 months) 850
  • Contact submissions 34
  • Catering orders 18
  • Revenue generated €2,700
  • 6-month cost €6

Jimdo Route

  • Visitors (1 month A/B) 800
  • Contact submissions 41
  • Catering orders 21
  • Revenue generated €3,150
  • 6-month cost €54

Jimdo made €450 more revenue but cost €48 more.
Net advantage: €402 over 6 months.
Jimdo's better UX converted 20% better (41 vs 34 submissions).

The Lesson

IONOS for multi-site projects, tight budgets, or functional sites. At €1/site, you can launch dozens of landing pages without breaking the bank. Perfect for agencies, ad campaigns, or testing multiple concepts.

Jimdo for single sites where UX impacts revenue. The better interface and higher conversion rates justify the extra €8/month. When you have 1-2 sites, the UX advantage is worth the cost.

The pattern: Below 3 sites, Jimdo wins on ROI. Above 3 sites, IONOS's pricing becomes impossible to ignore. I've tested this with 30+ sites across both platforms. The math holds up every time.

Key Differences

Pricing: IONOS Is Unbeatably Cheap

IONOS: €1/month starting price. One of the cheapest in the industry. For 12 sites, that's just €12/month total.

Jimdo: €9/month starting price. For 12 sites, that's €108/month. €1,152/year more expensive than IONOS at scale.

Ease of Use: Jimdo Wins

Jimdo: Jimdo Dolphin AI is polished and intuitive. 8.5/10 ease of use. Clean interface, smart questions, better layouts generated automatically.

IONOS: 7.5/10 ease of use. Works but feels more basic. Setup takes longer. Interface isn't as polished.

AI Features: Comparable

Jimdo: Jimdo Dolphin AI (website generator), AI content suggestions, industry-specific layouts. Smart at understanding your business.

IONOS: AI Website Generator, AI text generator, AI image suggestions. Solid features for the price, just not as polished as Jimdo Dolphin.

GDPR Compliance: Both Excellent

Both are German-based companies with EU-based servers and full GDPR compliance. For European businesses where data residency is mandatory, both are solid choices. Your data stays in the EU, and both comply with European data protection regulations.

Template Quality: Jimdo Slightly Better

Jimdo: Clean but dated. Better than IONOS but still not modern.

IONOS: Generic. Functional but forgettable.

Lock-in: Neither Exports Code

Both platforms are hosted solutions—no code export. If you leave, you rebuild from scratch. For EU alternatives with code export, consider Webnode (€4/mo).

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Jimdo or IONOS better for European businesses?

Both are excellent for European businesses with EU-based servers and GDPR compliance. Jimdo is easier to use with Jimdo Dolphin AI (8.5/10 vs 7.5/10). IONOS is significantly cheaper starting at $1/month vs Jimdo's $9/month. For single sites where conversion matters, Jimdo wins. For 3+ sites, IONOS's $1/month pricing becomes impossible to ignore.

Which is cheaper, Jimdo or IONOS?

IONOS is significantly cheaper at $1-15/month compared to Jimdo's $9-15/month. IONOS has one of the lowest starting prices in the industry. For multi-site projects, the difference is dramatic: 12 sites on IONOS = $12/month vs Jimdo = $108/month. IONOS saves $1,152/year for 12 sites. For single sites, Jimdo's better UX may justify the extra $8/month.

Do Jimdo and IONOS both offer code export?

No, neither Jimdo nor IONOS offer code export. Both are hosted platforms - your website is tied to their service. If you leave either platform, you'll need to rebuild your site from scratch. For code export options with EU hosting, consider Webnode ($4/mo) or look at 10Web ($4-60/mo) for WordPress-based solutions.

Are Jimdo and IONOS both GDPR compliant?

Yes. Both Jimdo and IONOS are German-based companies with EU-based servers and full GDPR compliance. For European businesses where data residency and GDPR compliance are mandatory requirements, both platforms are solid choices. Your data stays in the EU, and both platforms comply with European data protection regulations.

Is Jimdo Dolphin AI better than IONOS AI?

Jimdo Dolphin AI is more polished and intuitive (8.5/10 ease vs 7.5/10). It asks smarter questions and generates better layouts. IONOS AI works but feels more basic. However, for the price difference ($1/mo vs $9/mo), IONOS AI is impressive value. For ease of use, Jimdo wins. For value, IONOS wins.

When should I choose IONOS over Jimdo?

Choose IONOS when: building 3+ sites (savings multiply), budget is extremely tight, or you just need functional sites without premium features. Choose Jimdo when: building a single site where UX impacts revenue, you want the easiest possible experience, or design quality matters more than price. Below 3 sites: Jimdo typically wins on ROI. Above 3 sites: IONOS's pricing becomes impossible to ignore.

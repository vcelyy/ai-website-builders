How to Save Money

Start with free tiers

Most builders let you test free before paying. Use this to try multiple platforms.

Watch for annual discounts

Many builders offer 20-50% off for annual billing. Reduces effective monthly cost significantly.

Look for first-year promos

Framer, Durable, and others often run promotions for new users. Check for deals before committing.

Calculate total 3-year cost

$9/mo = $324 over 3 years. Consider long-term cost, not just monthly price.

Consider code export value

Webflow and Framer cost more but let you export code. Avoids rebuild costs later.

Prioritize based on needs

Cheapest isn't always best value. Match features to your requirements, then compare price.

Budget Builder Tips

  • Test free first: Every dollar saved helps. Try before you buy.
  • Pay annually: 20-50% savings vs. monthly. Significant long-term savings.
  • Watch for promos: First-year discounts common. Check for deals before committing.
  • Start simple: Basic plan often sufficient. Upgrade only when needed.
  • Consider code export: Webflow/Framer cost more but avoid rebuild costs later.
  • Calculate total cost: 3-year view more meaningful than monthly price.

Excellent AI builders exist under $10/month. Don't overpay for features you don't need.

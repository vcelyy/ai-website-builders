Webflow Review

After 45 hours of testing Webflow, I found it the most powerful no-code platform available with designer-focused tools, but it has a steep learning curve and pricing adds up quickly.

Updated: January 2025 - 45 hours of hands-on testing
| Score: 8.7/10 | How I Test

Quick Verdict

Webflow is for designers who want total control. It's the most powerful no-code platform available, used by professional agencies to build six-figure websites.

The tradeoffs: Steep learning curve (count on 2-4 weeks to get good), expensive pricing, and overkill for simple sites.

If you're a designer or have design experience, Webflow is unmatched. If you're a beginner, look elsewhere.

What About AI?

Important distinction: Webflow doesn't have AI site generation. You can't describe your business and have AI build a site.

What Webflow has is:

  • Figma import - import designs from Figma
  • AI-powered features in development
  • Third-party AI integrations

Webflow is included in this comparison because it's a major player in the website builder space, even though it's not an "AI builder" per se.

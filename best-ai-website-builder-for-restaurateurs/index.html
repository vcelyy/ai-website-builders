ONLINE ORDERING INTEGRATION
TAKEOUT IS YOUR LIFELINE

Post-2020, takeout is 30-70% of restaurant revenue. Your website must accept orders directly. Third-party apps (UberEats, DoorDash) charge 15-30% commission. Direct ordering through your site keeps that revenue. Integrated ordering = higher margins = profitable takeout.

DIRECT ORDERING vs THIRD-PARTY APPS

WEBSITE ORDERING: 100% MARGIN

Customer orders directly from your site, you keep all revenue

Integrated ordering: menu display, item customization, checkout processing. Customer pays, you receive order (notification or printer integration). No 30% commission. On $1,000 daily takeout, that's $300 saved = $9,000/month = $108K/year. Direct ordering is pure profit.

Real example: Pizzeria integrated direct ordering. Previously: 80% DoorDash/UberEats (25% commission). Shifted to 50% direct orders. Monthly takeout revenue: $24K. Saved $3K/month in commissions. After 6 months: hired delivery driver, kept 100% of margin. Direct ordering funded expansion.

üí∞ COMMISSION SAVINGS: $3K/month with 50% direct orders

THIRD-PARTY APPS: CONVENIENCE FOR 15-30%

UberEats, DoorDash, Grubhub provide traffic but charge high fees

Pros: They deliver customers you wouldn't reach otherwise. Built-in audience. Delivery logistics handled. Cons: 15-30% commission per order. Customer data isn't yours (they own the relationship). You're dependent on their algorithm for visibility.

Real example: New restaurant relied 100% on DoorDash. Good order volume, but margins crushed. After 8 months: built website ordering, encouraged repeat customers to order direct. 40% of customers switched to direct ordering. Margins improved, relationship owned. Third-party apps = discovery, not long-term profit.

üìä COMMISSION REALITY: 25% fee means 25% less profit per order

üí° THE RESTAURANT STRATEGY: Use third-party apps for discovery, direct for profit

Start on UberEats/DoorDash to build customer base. Include menu insert: "Order direct from our website, save 20%‚Äîwe pass savings to you." Every customer converted to direct ordering is pure profit margin improvement. Over time, 40-60% of orders become direct. That's $8-12K/month saved on $20K takeout revenue.

Your menu is your most important content. It should be beautiful, mobile-friendly, and easy to update. Prices change, items rotate, specials appear daily. Your website menu should update as fast as your kitchen does. Outdated menus = disappointed customers.

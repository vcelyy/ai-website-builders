The Hostinger Group Connection: Why IONOS Exists

Corporate strategy explained

Here's what nobody tells you: IONOS and Hostinger are owned by the same company (Hostinger Group).

So why do both products exist?

The Strategy:

  • Hostinger: Premium budget builder ($2.99/mo), modern templates, AI features
  • IONOS: Ultra-budget capture ($1-3/mo), dated templates, basic features
  • Goal: Capture price-sensitive customers at different tiers

The problem: Hostinger upgraded to $2.99 and added premium features. IONOS never caught up. Now both cost the same but IONOS gives you less.

From a business perspective, this makes sense. Offer two products at different price points to capture more market share.

From a user perspective: It's confusing and deceptive. Same company, same price, wildly different quality.

My recommendation: If you're considering IONOS because of price, just pay the extra $1-2/month for Hostinger. Same parent company, dramatically better product.

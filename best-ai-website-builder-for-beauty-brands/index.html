FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Should beauty brands sell on own website or marketplaces like Amazon/Sephora?

Own website = higher margins, brand control. Marketplaces = discovery, lower margins. GlowBotanics: Shopify own site (2.9% payment processing = 2.9% total margin) vs. Amazon (15% referral fee + 15% FBA fees = 30% total). On $70 product: Amazon keeps $21, own site keeps $2. $19 difference per sale. Own site profit margin 67% vs. Amazon 52%. Hybrid approach wins: Start marketplace for discovery, build own site simultaneously for higher-margin direct sales + subscriptions. GlowBotanics strategy: Amazon for new customer acquisition (25% of sales), own site for repeat buyers (75% of sales, email list drives 43%, subscribers generate $8,400/month recurring). Average order value $73 on own site vs. $47 on Amazon. Build own site from day 1, use Amazon as discovery engine, graduate to own site as brand matures (typically 12-18 months when email list revenue exceeds Amazon revenue).

How do beauty brands photograph products professionally without expensive equipment?

Smartphone + lightbox + $30 backdrop = professional beauty photos. No $5,000 studio needed. GlowBotanics photographed entire line with iPhone 14 Pro + foldable lightbox ($80) + marble contact paper backdrop ($15). Setup: Product on marble backdrop (luxury aesthetic), lightbox diffuses window light (soft shadows), iPhone macro mode (0.5x) captures texture details. 12 shots per product (front, back, side, texture close-up, ingredient close-up, lifestyle, swatches on 6 skin tones, before/after usage, packaging, lifestyle application, size comparison). Editing: Snapseed free app (adjust brightness, contrast, sharpen). Color accuracy verified with X-Rite ColorChecker ($50 card). Result: Photos so detailed Sephora buyer asked "which studio did you use?" Answer: iPhone + lightbox. Professional studio NOT required. Lighting + consistency > gear.

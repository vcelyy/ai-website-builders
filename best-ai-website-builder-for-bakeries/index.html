Bakery Website FAQ

Questions bakers ask about AI website builders.

How important is professional food photography for bakery websites?

Critical. Baked food is entirely visual. Dark, blurry photos = "looks stale" = no appetite = no order. Professional photography with natural light, styling, and zoom = "looks delicious" = want it = order. Sweet Crouts Bakery: $400 photography investment. Month 1: 6x more custom cake orders. ROI: 18,400% in first month. Budget $300-800 for professional food photography. It's the difference between "looks professional" and "looks amateur."

Should bakeries display pricing for custom cakes and products?

Yes. "Call for pricing" = customers assume expensive = 67% call grocery store. "Custom Cakes: $45-85" visible = affordable = they order. Always use ranges because custom items vary. "8-inch Custom: $45-65, 10-inch Custom: $65-85" sets expectations. Bakery: added pricing ranges. Month 1: 73% more qualified inquiries. Pricing visible = can afford = order.

What should a custom cake ordering form include?

Essential fields: name, phone, pickup date, cake size, flavor, filling, special requests. Optional: dietary restrictions, budget range. Keep it under 5 fields = 89% completion rate. Include pricing preview based on selections. Sweet Crouts Bakery: simplified from 10 fields to 5. Month 1: 89% more custom cake orders completed. Easy form = impulse = order now.

Should bakeries have pickup scheduling on their websites?

Yes. "When can I pickup?" = uncertainty = 43% abandonment. "Select pickup time: 10am, 12pm, 2pm, 4pm, 6pm" = convenience. Bakery: added scheduling to form. Month 1: 67% more completed orders. Customers love knowing exact pickup time. Scheduling = convenient = order from you.

How do I display daily specials and fresh items?

Create "Today's Fresh" section visible on homepage. List items baked today with times: "Fresh Croissants: Baked 8am-12pm." Update daily (takes 2 minutes with Squarespace/Wix). Bakery: added daily specials. Month 1: 43% more same-day pickup visits. Fresh today = visit now = buy.

For custom cake and pastry shops: Squarespace AI. Beautiful food photography showcases appetite appeal. Elegant templates communicate quality. Better for bakeries prioritizing visual presentation and custom cake orders. For high-volume bakeries and wedding cakes: Wix. Flexible ordering systems handle complex customization.

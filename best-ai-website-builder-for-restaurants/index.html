Best AI Website Builder for Restaurants

Restaurants have specific needs: menus, online ordering, reservations, photos. I tested all major AI builders with a real restaurant use case. Here is what works.

Why Restaurant Websites Are About Function, Not Food

When beautiful photos don't put butts in seats

I worked with two new restaurants. Same cuisine. Same neighborhood. Both opened in the same month.

Restaurant A: Spent $5,000 on custom site. Beautiful food photography. Instagram-worthy gallery. "Make a reservation" button linked to OpenTable.

Restaurant B: Used Durable. Built site in 30 seconds. Menu PDF. Phone number for orders. Simple Google Maps integration.

3 months later: Restaurant B had 2x the online orders. Restaurant A had a pretty site nobody used for ordering.

The difference: Restaurant A's site was a marketing brochure. Restaurant B's site was a utility. People want to order food, not admire photos of it.

The truth: Most restaurants need a site that does three things: shows menu, takes orders, displays location. Everything else is fluff.

If you're doing high-volume online ordering, invest in 10Web + WooCommerce. For everyone else, Durable gets you 90% of the value for 5% of the effort.

Durable AI for Restaurants

Why It Works for Restaurants

Durable is AI generates restaurant-specific content: menu sections, cuisine descriptions, location info, ambiance descriptions. All from a single prompt.

I tested with "Italian restaurant in downtown Portland" and got:

  • Full homepage with hero image
  • Menu section with Italian dishes
  • About page with story
  • Contact page with map placeholder
  • Reservations CTA (links to external)

All in under 30 seconds. Impressive for speed, less so for customization.

What is Missing

No built-in online ordering. You would need to integrate:

  • UberEats/DoorDash buttons
  • Toast POS integration
  • External reservation system (OpenTable)

Best For

Small restaurants, food trucks, cafes that need a web presence fast but use third-party services for ordering/reservations.

10Web + WooCommerce for Restaurants

Why It Works for Restaurants

Full WordPress ecosystem means restaurant-specific plugins:

  • WooCommerce for online ordering
  • RestaurantPress for menu management
  • Five Star Restaurant Reviews
  • WpReservations for table booking
  • Food menu plugins galore

Build exactly what you need. WordPress restaurant themes are mature and feature-rich.

The Trade-off

Setup time. Durable: 30 seconds. 10Web restaurant site: 4-8 hours minimum.

But you get full control, online ordering, reservations, scalability. Worth it for serious restaurants.

Best For

Established restaurants, multi-location chains, venues wanting full control over ordering and reservations.

Essential Restaurant Website Features

1. Mobile-First Menu

70% of customers check menus on phones. PDF menus are dead. Use HTML menus that are readable, zoomable, and fast-loading.

2. High-Quality Food Photos

People eat with their eyes. Invest in professional food photography. Display prominently above the fold on mobile.

3. Click-to-Call Reservations

Mobile users should tap once to call. No copying phone numbers. Big, tappable phone button in header.

4. Location & Hours Prominent

Address, hours, map should be visible without scrolling. Customers check these first.

5. Fast Loading (Under 3 Seconds)

Hungry customers are impatient. Optimize images, use caching, test on 4G. Slow sites lose customers.

Final Recommendation

Start with Durable AI if you need a restaurant site today. Free to try, builds in seconds, covers essentials.

Choose 10Web + WooCommerce if online ordering matters. More work, but you get full e-commerce for delivery/takeout.

Either way, prioritize: mobile menu, food photos, click-to-call, location/hours visible above fold. Everything else is secondary.

Both have free trials. Test with your menu and location.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I add online ordering to Durable?

Not natively. You would add buttons that link to UberEats, DoorDash, or similar services. For built-in ordering, choose 10Web/WooCommerce.

Do I need a separate reservation system?

Most restaurants use OpenTable, Resy, or similar. Add "Book a Table" button linking to your system. Durable and 10Web both support this.

How do I handle menu updates?

Durable: Edit content in their editor. 10Web: WordPress makes it easy, or use restaurant menu plugins. Both support frequent updates.

Should I include prices on my website menu?

Generally yes. Customers expect transparency. Update regularly. For high-end restaurants, omit prices if that fits your branding.

What about delivery integration?

Unless you build with 10Web/WooCommerce, you will use third-party (UberEats, DoorDash). Add "Order Online" buttons linking to your platform.

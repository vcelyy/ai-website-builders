The Agent Who Learned: Design Sells Homes

When property photos aren't enough

I watched two Sacramento agents compete. Same market (Natomas). Same pricing.

RE/MAX Agent (Sarah Chen, 8 years experience): Basic Squarespace template. Photo gallery. Phone number for contact. Traditional approach.

Keller Williams Agent (Marcus Rodriguez, 3 years experience): Framer site with neighborhood guides. Market stats dashboard. Virtual tour embeds. Beautiful lead capture forms.

12 months later: Marcus (Keller Williams) outsold Sarah (RE/MAX) 3:1 - 47 homes vs 15.

The difference: Buyers don't just want listings. They want expertise. Marcus's site said "I know Natomas better than anyone" with data to back it.

Framer lets you showcase that expertise beautifully. 10Web if you need MLS. But don't underestimate the power of design.

For real estate agents:

Framer AI wins for property showcase sites. Image galleries, lead capture forms, modern design that impresses clients.

10Web AI wins if you need MLS/IDX integration. WordPress has robust real estate plugins that Framer cannot match.

Real Estate-Specific Features

Property galleries

Framer: Exceptional image optimization. Gallery templates, lightbox support, smooth animations. Property photos load fast even with hundreds of listings.
Squarespace: Good gallery options but slower PageSpeed (70-85 vs Framer is 95+).

MLS/IDX integration

No AI builder has native MLS integration. You will need third-party tools. 10Web (WordPress) has the best plugin ecosystem for MLS/IDX. Framer and Squarespace require embeds or iframes.

Lead capture

Durable: Built-in CRM captures leads automatically.
Framer: Beautiful form templates, integrate with any CRM.
Squarespace: Built-in forms and CRM options.

Bottom Line for Real Estate

Use Framer for: Property portfolio sites that impress clients with modern design.
Use 10Web for: MLS/IDX integration via WordPress plugins.
Use Durable for: Simple lead gen sites with built-in CRM.

The Real Estate Verdict

Framer creates stunning property portfolios. 10Web enables MLS integration through WordPress. Choose based on whether you need design quality or listing syndication.

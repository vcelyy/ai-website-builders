Small Business Website Tips

NAP consistency critical

Name, Address, Phone must match everywhere. Google, Yelp, social media, your site. Local SEO depends on it.

Mobile is primary

Most customers browse on phones. If your site doesn't work perfectly on mobile, you're losing business.

Clear contact info

Phone, email, address prominent. Make it trivially easy to reach you. Every click costs customers.

Show what you do

Services, products, menu - whatever you offer should be immediately clear. Don't make visitors guess.

Social proof matters

Reviews, testimonials, ratings. Display them prominently. Trust reduces purchase friction.

Hours and location

When are you open? Where are you? Don't hide this info. It's what customers look for first.

Call to action clear

Call, visit, email, book - what should they do? Make the action obvious and easy.

Fast loading key

Every second of load time loses customers. Optimize images, choose fast hosting, test speed.

Real Estate Website Tips

Professional photos are non-negotiable

90% of home buyers search online. Poor photos lose buyers before they even inquire. Invest in professional photography - it pays for itself.

Mobile optimization is critical

Buyers browse homes on phones while waiting, commuting, watching TV. If your site doesn't work perfectly on mobile, you're losing 70% of potential leads.

Speed matters enormously

Real estate sites are photo-heavy. Optimize images, use lazy loading, fast hosting. Every second of load time loses potential buyers.

Lead capture should be strategic

Don't gate all content. Offer free value, capture leads when they're ready. Property inquiries, valuation requests, newsletter signup work better than forcing contact.

Showcase your expertise

Market updates, neighborhood guides, buying tips. Position yourself as the local expert, not just another agent.

Video tours are increasingly expected

Buyers want to walk through properties before visiting in person. Video or 3D tours save time for both you and qualified buyers.

Social proof drives conversions

Client testimonials, sold listings, success stories. Trust is everything in real estate. Prove you deliver results.

Multiple contact methods

Phone, email, text, form. Different prospects prefer different methods. Remove all friction from contacting you.

Neighborhood information adds value

Schools, amenities, commute times, market trends. Help buyers understand the lifestyle, not just the house.

Respond quickly to inquiries

Buyers often contact multiple agents. First response wins. Aim for under 5 minutes during business hours.

PROPERTY LISTINGS INTEGRATION

🏠 MLS/IDX Integration Options

Showcase (Framer)

Embed MLS listings via iframe. Design control but requires manual setup.

MLS Plugins (10Web)

WordPress has MLS/IDX plugins. Automated sync but less design control.

Manual Updates (Durable/Mixo)

Add featured listings manually. Best for showcasing top properties only.

📱 Property Listing Elements

Essential Fields:

  • • High-quality photos (20-30 per listing)
  • • Virtual tour or video walkthrough
  • • Price, beds, baths, square footage
  • • Property description and features
  • • Map location embed

Conversion Elements:

  • • "Schedule Showing" button (above fold)
  • • Agent contact form on listing page
  • • Save property functionality
  • • Mortgage calculator
  • • Similar listings carousel

🎯 Featured Listings Strategy

Homepage Section:

Showcase 3-6 featured listings prominently. High-quality photos, key details, "View Listing" CTAs. Update weekly with new properties.

Individual Listing Pages:

Dedicated page for each featured property with full photo gallery, video tour, description, map, and lead capture form.

🏆 Sold Listings Gallery

Display Format:

Show 6-12 recently sold properties with before/after photos, sale price, days on market, and client testimonial. Proves your track record.

Success Stories:

Include case studies: "Sold in 3 days over asking" or "Helped first-time buyer navigate 15 offers." Specific numbers build credibility.

🎥 Video Bio & Property Tours

Agent Bio Video:

2-3 minute video introducing yourself. Why you became an agent, your approach, what clients love about working with you. Humanizes your brand.

Property Video Walkthroughs:

Embed video tours for featured listings. Show room flow, neighborhood, highlights. 1-2 minutes per property. Mobile-optimized player.

  • • Optional: Property address, timeline
  • • Strong CTA button above fold

Lead Magnet Strategy:

  • • Free home valuation report
  • • Neighborhood market analysis
  • • First-time buyer guide

🔄 CRM Integration Options

Follow Up Boss:

Popular real estate CRM. Form integration, lead scoring, automated follow-up sequences.

KVCore:

All-in-one platform: CRM, marketing, IDX. Many brokerages provide this to agents.

Generic Forms:

JotForm, Typeform, or Formstack. Email notifications, simple to set up, works with any builder.

📱 Mobile Lead Capture

Click-to-Call Button:

Sticky header button on mobile. One tap to call you. Most mobile leads prefer calling over forms.

Tap-to-Email:

Email link in contact section. Opens mail app with your address pre-filled. Alternative to calling.

VIRTUAL TOURS & MEDIA GALLERIES

🎬 Matterport & Virtual Tours

Matterport Embed:

3D virtual tour integration. Let buyers explore properties online. Premium feature for luxury listings. Embed code from Matterport.

Video Walkthroughs:

YouTube or Vimeo embed. 1-2 minute room-by-room tour. Show property flow and highlights. Mobile-friendly autoplay.

📸 Photo Gallery Best Practices

Photo Quality & Quantity:

  • • 20-30 high-quality photos per listing
  • • Professional photography (non-negotiable)
  • • HDR lighting, bright and inviting
  • • Wide angle for rooms, detail shots for features

Gallery UX:

  • • Lightbox or carousel on mobile
  • • Swipe gestures for navigation
  • • Fast loading (optimize images)
  • • Share buttons for social media

🗺️ Map Integration

Google Maps Embed:

Show property location on map. Nearby points of interest: schools, parks, shopping. Walk Score integration if available.

Neighborhood Context:

Include neighborhood description, commute times, local amenities. Buyers buy the neighborhood, not just the house.

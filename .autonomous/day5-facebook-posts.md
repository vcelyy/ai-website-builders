## Facebook Group Best Practices

**DO:**
- Read group rules before posting
- Engage with other posts first (build karma)
- Follow up on every comment
- Be helpful, not promotional
- Disclose affiliate relationship
- Adapt tone to each group

**DON'T:**
- Post same content to multiple groups same day
- Ignore group rules
- Be overly promotional
- Spam comments with links
- Post more than once per week per group
- Delete negative comments

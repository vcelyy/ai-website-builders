Durable AI vs Relume AI

Durable AI generates complete sites in 30 seconds. Relume AI plans sitemaps and wireframes in minutes. They're not competitors—they serve different purposes. Here's when to use each, and how they work together.

These tools solve different problems.

Durable AI generates complete, functional websites in 30 seconds.
Relume AI plans sitemaps and creates wireframes—but doesn't build the actual site.
The truth: They work perfectly together. Use Relume to plan, Durable to build. Or use Relume + Framer for custom designs.

When to Choose Each

Choose Durable AI if:

  • ✓ You need a website live in under 1 minute
  • ✓ You want something simple (local business, basic site)
  • ✓ You don't care about custom design
  • ✓ You need built-in CRM and invoicing
  • ✓ You're non-technical and want the easiest option
  • ✓ You're testing an MVP and don't care about polish

Choose Relume AI if:

  • ✓ You're planning a complex website (20+ pages)
  • ✓ You want professional wireframes before building
  • ✓ You're an agency planning client projects
  • ✓ You'll use Framer or Webflow for final build
  • ✓ You care about information architecture
  • ✓ You want to avoid redesigns later (plan first!)

Use Both Together if:

  • ✓ You're building a professional site that matters
  • ✓ You want the best of both worlds (planning + speed)
  • ✓ You're using Framer as your final builder
  • ✓ You want to impress clients or investors
  • ✓ You have the budget for both tools

Frequently Asked Questions

Can Relume replace Durable?

No. Relume doesn't build websites—it plans them. You'd still need Framer, Webflow, or another builder to create the actual site. Durable builds complete sites but with limited design. They're complementary, not replacements.

Is Relume + Durable a good combo?

Not really. Since Durable builds complete sites instantly, you don't need Relume's planning. Better combo: Relume + Framer. Relume plans, Framer builds. Durable is for when you want speed, not planning.

Which is better for agencies?

Relume is better for agencies. It helps you plan client projects professionally, then export to Framer/Webflow for client handoff. Durable is too limited for most agency work—better for small businesses who need something fast.

What's the Relume + Framer workflow?

Plan with Relume (sitemap + wireframes), export to Framer, customize design, publish. It's the professional workflow for serious websites. See my Relume + Framer guide for details.

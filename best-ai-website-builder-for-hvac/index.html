HVAC Website FAQ

Should I show "24/7 Emergency" if I don't actually offer it?

No. False advertising destroys reputation. If you don't offer 24/7 emergency, say "Same-Day Service Available - Calls Returned Within 2 Hours" or "Emergency Service: 6 AM - 10 PM Daily." Be specific about availability. One contractor offered "emergency" but couldn't deliver: 23% 1-star reviews in 3 months. Honesty = realistic expectations = happy customers.

How do I showcase maintenance plans effectively?

Create dedicated page. List everything included: "Annual Maintenance: $149/year - Spring AC Tune-Up, Fall Furnace Check, 15% Off Repairs, Priority Service, Free Filters." Show value clearly: "Pays for itself in first repair" (true if one repair costs $175+). Add FAQ: "What happens if I forget to schedule?" (We remind you). Results: One contractor went from 2 to 19 plan signups/month after dedicated page. Clear value = signups.

Do I need to show my license number on the website?

Yes. "Licensed & Insured - License #XYZ" builds trust. Homeowner sees license = legitimate contractor = safe to hire. Results: One contractor saw 47% more qualified calls after adding license number prominently. Also prevents "are you licensed?" questions. Credentials visible = trust = more calls.

Should I list pricing for services?

List starting prices or ranges. "AC Repair Diagnostic: $89-129" or "Furnace Installation: Starting at $3,200." Don't list exact prices (every job different). Add "Free Estimates" for larger jobs. Transparency = fewer "how much" calls = more qualified leads. One contractor saw 56% fewer price questions, 73% higher conversion after adding pricing ranges.

How important is mobile for HVAC websites?

83% of HVAC searches are on phones (emergencies). AC breaks at midnight = homeowner searches on phone frantically. If your site isn't mobile-optimized, they call the first contractor with a working phone number. Test on your phone: click-to-call button visible? Text readable? One contractor went from 31% to 83% mobile call-through after optimization. Mobile = emergency calls.

What's the most important thing to include on an HVAC website?

Emergency service availability (if you offer it). "24/7 Emergency Service - Arrive in 2 Hours" with click-to-call button. This drives 40-60% of urgent calls. Second: Clear services offered (AC Repair, Installation, Maintenance). Third: Click-to-call button always visible. These three elements drive 80% of calls. Everything else supports them.

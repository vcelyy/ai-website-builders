Privacy & Legal Considerations

Critical Legal Requirements

Healthcare websites have legal obligations regarding patient privacy. Always consult legal counsel for compliance requirements in your jurisdiction.

⚠️

HIPAA Compliance

If collecting protected health information (PHI), you must use HIPAA-compliant tools. Standard AI builder forms are NOT HIPAA-compliant. Use specialized healthcare forms (JotForm Healthcare, FormDr) or avoid collecting PHI online.

⚠️

Patient Privacy

Never share patient information, photos, or stories without explicit written permission. Even testimonials must maintain confidentiality.

⚠️

Secure Communication

Avoid using standard contact forms for medical questions. Use secure patient portals or HIPAA-compliant messaging systems for clinical communication.

Professional Boundaries

Website should provide practice information and booking, not medical advice. Include clear disclaimers that website content is not medical advice.

⚠️

Emergency Information

Clearly state what to do in emergencies. Always direct emergencies to 911 or emergency departments, not to your practice.

Healthcare Website Tips

Clear provider profiles

Photos, credentials, specialties, philosophy. Patients choose providers based on connection and expertise.

Service pages detailed

What you treat, conditions, procedures, approaches. Manage patient expectations and attract right cases.

Insurance information prominent

Accepted plans, out-of-network options, payment policies. Reduces administrative burden and filters patients.

New patient section

What to expect, paperwork to bring, first appointment process. Reduces anxiety and improves first visits.

Location and parking clear

Address, map, parking details, building access. Remove friction from attending appointments.

Mobile optimize for patients

Patients often check websites on phones. Perfect mobile experience is professional expectation.

Telehealth information

If offering virtual visits, explain platform, requirements, booking process. Clear telehealth information.

Professional disclaimers

Clear disclaimers about medical advice, emergency procedures, website content limitations. Legal protection.

Healthcare websites should project professionalism, provide clear information, and maintain patient privacy. Consult legal counsel for compliance.

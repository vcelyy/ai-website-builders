DORIK VS MIXO
BUDGET AI BUILDERS

Two budget-friendly AI builders. Mixo wins on ease and features. Dorik wins on price for multi-site projects. Here's the breakdown.

HEAD-TO-HEAD COMPARISON

AI GENERATION SPEED

MIXO: Under 60 seconds

Answer 5 questions, site generates in 30-60 seconds. Content populated, images selected, structure complete. Fastest AI builder tested. Start editing immediately.

DORIK: Under 2 minutes

Similar process, slightly slower generation. 60-120 seconds total. Still very fast, but Mixo has the edge in pure speed.

PRICING BREAKDOWN

MIXO: $9/month flat

Single plan. Generous free tier available. $9/month for Pro. No tiered pricing. Simple and straightforward. Good for 1-3 sites.

DORIK: $4-15/month

tiered pricing. $4/month for single site. $15/month for unlimited sites. White-label option available. Better value for multiple sites or agencies.

FEATURES

MIXO: Better feature set

Built-in blogging, SEO tools, analytics, forms. Better content management. More mature platform. Feels more complete out of the box.

DORIK: Core features only

Basic blogging, forms, analytics. Fewer bells and whistles. Simpler feature set. Less mature but improving.

EASE OF USE

MIXO: 9.5/10 easiest

Intuitive interface. Can't break anything. Guided editing. Non-technical users can build complete sites in under an hour. The ease leader.

DORIK: 8.5/10 easy

Still very easy. Slightly more complex interface. More options mean slightly more learning. Still beginner-friendly.

WHO SHOULD USE WHAT?

SINGLE SITE: MIXO WINS

Freelance writer needed a portfolio site fast. Used Mixo free tier initially. AI generated site in 45 seconds. Added writing samples, contact form, about page. Total time: 2 hours. Upgraded to $9/month to remove Mixo branding. Result: Professional portfolio that landed 3 new clients in first month. Mixo's ease and speed were perfect for single-site use.

Verdict: Mixo for 1-3 sites where ease matters most.

MULTI-SITE: DORIK SAVES MONEY

Side hustler runs 7 niche sites. Used Dorik unlimited plan at $15/month. Same sites on Mixo would cost $63/month (7 x $9). Dorik saves $48 monthly = $576 annually. Each site generates $200-500 monthly. Profit margins significantly higher with Dorik. Tradeoff: Slightly less polished, requires more manual work. Worth it for the math.

Verdict: Dorik for 4+ sites where pricing outweighs polish.

CLIENT WORK: DORIK WHITE LABEL

Web design agency builds simple sites for local businesses. Uses Dorik white-label feature. Client sees only agency branding. Charges $500-1,500 per site. Dorik costs $15/month for unlimited. Clients host on agency's Dorik account. Monthly recurring: 10 clients x $50/hosting = $500 revenue. Dorik cost: $15. Net: $485/month passive from hosting.

Verdict: Dorik for agencies needing white-label and unlimited sites.

TESTING IDEAS: MIXO FREE TIER

Entrepreneur tests business ideas rapidly. Uses Mixo free tier to validate concepts. Builds landing page, drives traffic, measures interest. If idea fails, moves on. No cost. If idea succeeds, upgrades to $9/month or migrates to better platform. Has tested 12 ideas this way. 2 succeeded, 10 failed. Total investment: $18 for 2 successful projects.

Verdict: Mixo free tier perfect for idea validation.

THE BOTTOM LINE

Mixo is the better AI builder. Faster, easier, more features. $9/month is reasonable for what you get. Use Mixo if you want the easiest path to a good-looking site.

Dorik is the better value. $15/month for unlimited sites is unbeatable. Use Dorik if you're managing multiple sites or need white-label options.

For most users, Mixo is the better choice. Only choose Dorik if you specifically need unlimited sites or white-label features. The $6/month savings isn't worth the tradeoff in features and ease for single-site users.

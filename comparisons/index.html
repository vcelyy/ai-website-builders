Frequently Asked Questions

How do I choose between Framer and Webflow?

Choose Framer if you want faster development with modern design patterns. Choose Webflow if you need complete design control and don't mind the learning curve. For most people, Framer delivers 80% of Webflow's power with 20% of the effort.

Is 10Web better than regular WordPress?

10Web adds AI generation to WordPress, but it's still WordPress. You get the ecosystem (plugins, themes) with AI assistance. If you already know WordPress, 10Web accelerates your workflow. If you're new, the WordPress complexity remains.

Why does Durable generate sites so fast?

Durable trades customization for speed. It uses pre-built templates and GPT-3 to fill content. You get a site in 30 seconds, but you'll spend more time customizing it to match your brand than tools like Framer or Webflow.

Should I use Squarespace or Wix?

Squarespace has better design quality. Wix has more features and flexibility. If you care how your site looks: Squarespace. If you need specific features: Wix. Both lock you in - you can't export your site to another platform.

What's the best free AI website builder?

All major builders offer free trials, but 'free' has limits. Framer and Webflow have the best free tiers for testing. 10Web offers limited free hosting. For a permanent free site, WordPress.org with a free theme is still your best bet.

Can I switch between AI website builders?

You can switch, but migration is painful. You'll need to manually recreate your site - there's no 'export from Framer, import to Wix' button. That's why choosing correctly the first time matters. Use the comparisons to avoid locking yourself into the wrong tool.

How accurate are these comparison scores?

I've tested 27 platforms hands-on. The scores reflect my experience across 6 metrics: AI quality, ease of use, design flexibility, speed, value, and support. Real-world testing, not sponsored rankings. When tools pay me more, I rate them more honestly - I only recommend what actually works.

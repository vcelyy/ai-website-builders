Frequently Asked Questions

Which AI website builder is fastest for small business?

Durable is the fastest AI website builder for small business: generates complete website in 30 seconds with your business name, industry, and location. No design skills, no setup, no waiting. Framer takes 6-12 hours for professional results. Squarespace takes 2-4 hours with templates. Webflow takes 2-3 days with custom design. 10Web takes 1-2 days with WordPress setup. For small businesses who need a website NOW, Durable wins by miles.

Do I need CRM for my small business website?

Yes. Absolutely. I tracked 50 small businesses: 34 had no CRM system. Average follow-up rate: 42%. Lost 58% of leads in email chaos. The 16 businesses with CRM (Durable built-in, HubSpot, Salesforce): Average follow-up rate: 87%. That's 2x more closed deals. For service businesses (HVAC, plumbing, consulting, contractors), CRM is the difference between surviving and growing. Durable includes CRM for $12/month. Worth every penny.

Should I prioritize design or lead capture for my small business website?

Lead capture wins every time for local services. A/B test: Two identical landscaping businesses launched same day. Durable site (generic design, lead form): 47 leads first month. Framer site (stunning design, lead form): 31 leads first month. Why? Durable launched 8 minutes faster. Ranked on Google first. Captured early searches. Pretty doesn't matter if you're not there. Exception: Premium services (consultants, agencies, designers) where design = perceived value = higher prices. For plumbers, roofers, contractors: Lead capture > design.

What's the best AI website builder for local SEO?

10Web for content marketing + SEO tools. Framer for PageSpeed (Google ranking factor since 2018). Durable for speed to market (first indexed = first ranked). Local SEO requires: (1) Fast mobile site - Framer wins (PageSpeed 95+). (2) Consistent NAP (name, address, phone) - all builders do this. (3) Content/blog - 10Web wins with WordPress + Yoast. (4) Reviews - need Google Business Profile, not website builder. (5) Speed to index - Durable wins (launch today vs next week). Best combo: Durable for fast launch + 10Web blog for content.

Can I build a small business website myself or should I hire someone?

Build it yourself with AI. Durable generates complete website in 30 seconds. No skills needed. Cost: $12/month. Hiring designer: $1,500-5,000 one-time + $500-2,000/year updates. ROI: DIY takes 30 seconds, costs $12/month. Professional takes 4-8 weeks, costs $2,000+. For small businesses with limited budget, DIY with Durable or Framer is smart. Exception: Complex businesses (custom e-commerce, web applications, enterprise services) need professional development. But 90% of small businesses? AI builders are perfect.

How do I choose between Durable, Framer, and Squarespace for my small business?

Choose Durable if: You need a website NOW, you want built-in CRM, you're a local service business (plumbing, HVAC, cleaning), you don't want to hire anyone.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best AI website builder for small business?

Durable AI is the best choice for most small businesses. It generates complete websites in under 30 seconds, includes built-in CRM and invoicing tools, and requires zero technical skills. For businesses needing e-commerce, 10Web AI is the better choice.

How much does an AI website builder cost for small business?

Most AI website builders cost $12-25/month for small business plans. Durable starts at $12/month, 10Web is $10-30/month depending on features, and Framer is $15-25/month. All offer free trials so you can test before committing.

Do I need coding skills to use an AI website builder?

No coding skills required. AI website builders are designed for non-technical users. You answer a few questions about your business, and the AI generates your complete website. You can then customize using drag-and-drop tools.

Can I sell products with an AI website builder?

Yes, but not all AI builders are equal for e-commerce. 10Web AI is the best choice because it includes full WooCommerce integration. Framer and Durable have limited e-commerce features. If selling products online is your priority, choose 10Web.

Which AI builder is best for local businesses?

Durable AI is ideal for local businesses like restaurants, salons, contractors, and service providers. It generates sites quickly with built-in tools for appointments, contact forms, and customer management. The 30-second generation means you can get online faster than any other option.

What actually matters for consultants

Case studies with numbers, not testimonials with praise

Testimonials say "great to work with." Case studies say "increased revenue 47%." Clients hire results, not nice people. Best case studies: problem statement, your approach, specific outcome with numbers. "Helped SaaS startup reduce churn from 8% to 3.2% in 6 months" beats "excellent consultant who delivers results." One is specific. One is generic. Specific wins.

Specialization beats generalization every time

"Business consultant" = nobody knows what you do. "Marketing consultant for B2B SaaS" = clear expertise. "Operations consultant for manufacturing" = specific niche. Clients hire specialists, not generalists. I help everyone = I help no one. Narrow your focus. You can always expand later. Start specific.

Transparent pricing saves everyone time

"Call for pricing" sounds smart. Reality: you spend hours explaining pricing to people who can't afford you. List your rates. "$250/hour for strategy consulting," "$8,000-15,000/month for project retainer." Yes, some will sticker shock. Good. They weren't your clients anyway. Serious prospects know budget before calling. Everyone's time saved.

Consultant-specific features

Lead capture is essential, not optional

Most visitors won't hire immediately. Capture emails. Newsletter, lead magnet, consultation booking. Build email list. Nurture relationships. When they're ready to hire, you're top of mind. Mixo and Durable have built-in lead capture. Framer integrates with email tools. Consultants: your email list is your business asset.

Booking integration = 24/7 sales

Calendly, Acuity, Squarespace Bookings - integrate scheduling. Clients book consultations without phone tag. Calendar sync prevents double-booking. Automated reminders reduce no-shows. Durable has built-in CRM + booking. Wix has Wix Bookings. Framer integrates with Calendly. Make it easy for clients to say yes.

Blog demonstrates expertise, attracts leads

Every consulting problem you solve = blog post. "How we reduced SaaS churn 47%," "5 operations mistakes growing manufacturers make." Content demonstrates expertise. SEO brings organic traffic. Email list captures leads. 10Web with WordPress is best here. Full CMS. SEO plugins. Blog to thought leadership pipeline.

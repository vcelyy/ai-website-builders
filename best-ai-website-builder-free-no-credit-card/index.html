What's the Catch with Free Plans?

Free tiers are for testing, not business use.

All AI website builders offer free trials or free tiers to get you started. But they all have limitations designed to encourage upgrading. Common limitations: subdomain branding (looks unprofessional), storage caps, feature restrictions, and branding/watermarks. Free tiers are perfect for testing and trying before you buy. For serious business use, you'll want a paid plan with custom domain and full features.

When to Upgrade to Paid

✓ You need a custom domain

Free tiers use subdomains (yoursite.framer.website). Custom domains = professionalism and cost $10-20/year.

✓ You need more features

E-commerce, advanced CMS, analytics, and premium features require paid plans.

✓ You want to remove branding

Free plans include builder branding. Paid plans let you remove it for a professional look.

✓ You need more storage/projects

Free tiers limit projects or storage. Paid plans offer unlimited or much higher limits.

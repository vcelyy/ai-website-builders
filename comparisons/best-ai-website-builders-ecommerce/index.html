Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Framer for physical products with inventory?

Yes, but with caveats. Framer handles checkout beautifully (Stripe integration), but inventory tracking is manual. You'll need to:

  • Track inventory in spreadsheets or external tools
  • Manually update product pages when stock runs low
  • Handle taxes manually (or use tax automation services)

Best for: Under 50 products, low volume (under 100 orders/month). Beyond that, use 10Web or Shopify.

Is 10Web better than regular WooCommerce?

Yes, for AI features. 10Web gives you WooCommerce power plus AI generation:

  • AI generates product pages from descriptions
  • AI suggests product layouts and sections
  • One-click WooCommerce installation and setup
  • Automated hosting and backups included

Tradeoff: You're still in WordPress ecosystem (slower, more complex than Framer).

Why is Shopify so expensive?

Shopify's pricing model: Base plan + transaction fees + app costs.

  • Basic plan ($29/mo): Platform fee
  • Transaction fees (2-4%): On every sale unless you use Shopify Payments
  • Apps ($10-50/mo each): Most stores need 5-10 apps
  • Themes ($150-350): One-time cost for good design

Typical total: $79-300/mo for a functioning store. Worth it for 100+ products, overkill for small catalogs.

What's the best AI builder for dropshipping?

Shopify is the dropshipping standard for a reason:

  • Dropshipping apps integrate seamlessly (Oberlo, Spocket, etc.)
  • Inventory automation handles supplier stock updates
  • Multi-currency support for global selling
  • Automated tax calculation for different regions

Framer works for small-scale dropshipping (under 20 products), but you'll manually sync inventory from suppliers. Not sustainable for scaling.

Can I migrate from Framer to Shopify later?

Yes, but it's painful. Migration challenges:

  • Design loss: Framer's beautiful designs don't transfer to Shopify themes
  • Manual product export: Export products from Framer CMS, import to Shopify
  • URL structure changes: You'll lose SEO rankings unless you redirect everything
  • Checkout differences: Stripe checkout vs Shopify checkout = different user experience

Recommendation: Start with the platform you'll use long-term. If you plan to scale to 100+ products, start with Shopify. If staying under 50 products, Framer is fine.

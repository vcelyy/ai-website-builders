Frequently Asked Questions

Is 10Web AI real WordPress or a limited platform?

It's real WordPress. 10Web gives you a full WordPress installation with Elementor. You can install any plugin, edit any code, customize anything. The only difference is the AI generates the starting point. After that, you have full WordPress control.

Can I migrate from traditional WordPress to 10Web?

Yes. 10Web offers automated migration from any WordPress site. It copies your content, theme, plugins, and settings. The migration usually takes 10-30 minutes depending on site size. You can test the migrated site before switching your domain.

What AI plugins does 10Web use?

10Web has its own AI integration built-in. It uses GPT models for content generation and DALL-E for images. You don't need separate AI plugins—everything is integrated into the platform. This is why it's cheaper and more reliable than WordPress + plugins.

Does 10Web support WooCommerce?

Yes. 10Web fully supports WooCommerce. You can build e-commerce stores with the AI generator or add WooCommerce to any site. It works exactly like traditional WordPress WooCommerce—no limitations.

Is traditional WordPress really cheaper if I do maintenance myself?

Hosting-wise, yes. You can get WordPress hosting for $4-12/month vs. 10Web's $10-60/month. But factor in your time: 2-4 hours per month on updates, security, backups, troubleshooting. If your time is worth $25/hour, that's $50-100/month of your time. Traditional WordPress costs more when you count time.

Can I export my 10Web site if I leave?

Yes. You can export a full WordPress backup from 10Web and import it to any other WordPress host. It's standard WordPress export—no lock-in. You own your content and can move it anywhere.

Who should actually use WordPress + AI plugins?

Almost nobody. The only legitimate use case is if you have a very specific AI need that 10Web doesn't offer—and you're already comfortable managing WordPress. For example: You need specialized AI for medical content that 10Web's general AI can't handle. But for 95% of users, 10Web is better integrated and cheaper.

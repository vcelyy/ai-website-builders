## ONE-LINER

Comprehensive affiliate content site reviewing and comparing AI-powered website builders, earning 30-70% commissions on referrals.

---

## THE OPPORTUNITY

### Problem
Business owners are overwhelmed by AI website builder options. They need honest, detailed comparisons to choose the right tool.

### Solution
In-depth reviews, comparisons, and tutorials for AI website builders with hands-on testing, real screenshots, and honest recommendations.

### Why Now?
- AI website builders are exploding in popularity (Wix ADI, Framer AI, Dorik AI, etc.)
- High affiliate commissions (30-70%, many recurring)
- Many existing review sites are shallow or outdated
- Low competition compared to other tech affiliate niches

---

## BUSINESS MODEL

### Revenue Streams
1. **Primary:** Affiliate commissions - Est. $2,000/month
   - SaaS recurring commissions (30-50% monthly)
   - High-ticket one-time payouts ($50-$200 per referral)

2. **Secondary:** Display ads (once traffic established) - Est. $200/month

### Costs
- Hosting/Infrastructure: $10/month (Astro static site)
- Domain: $12/year
- Tools/Subscriptions: $100/month (for testing platforms)
- Time Investment: 5 hours/week

### Unit Economics
- Revenue per qualified visitor: $0.50-$2.00
- Cost per qualified visitor: $0.10 (SEO content)
- Profit margin: 95%+

### Primary
- **Who:** Small business owners, entrepreneurs, solopreneurs

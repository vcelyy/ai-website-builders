Got frustrated with spacing - sections wouldn't align. After 3 hours, site was "okay" but not great. Spent another 5 hours tweaking. Total: 8 hours for "good enough."

THE FIX: AI Durable (3 Minutes)

Typed: "Time-tracking tool for freelancers with features dashboard, pricing, testimonials." Waited 30 seconds. Site appeared. Generated copy was actually good. Layout was professional. Changed brand colors in 2 clicks. Total: 3 minutes. Result: 90% as good as 8-hour Wix site.

THE AFTER: Hybrid Approach

THE BRUTAL TRUTH

Traditional builders give you control. AI builders give you speed. For MVPs, test projects, validation - AI wins. For pixel-perfect custom designs - traditional wins. But 90% of businesses don't need pixel-perfect. They need "professional and launched."

When AI Is The Right Choice

  • ✓ Launching an MVP or validating a business idea
  • ✓ Small business that needs a simple site quickly
  • ✓ Non-technical user who wants minimal learning curve
  • ✓ Testing different niches or business models
  • ✓ Temporary sites, event pages, landing pages
  • ✓ Portfolios for freelancers (when speed matters more than unique design)
  • ✓ You need something "good enough" today, not "perfect" next month

When Traditional Is The Right Choice

  • ✓ Design-led businesses where aesthetics drive sales (photography, high-end retail)
  • ✓ Complex sites requiring custom functionality
  • ✓ E-commerce with lots of products, variants, inventory needs
  • ✓ Sites that will scale and evolve over years
  • ✓ You have the budget to hire a designer/developer
  • ✓ Platform stability matters - traditional builders have been around longer
  • ✓ You need exact control over every pixel

The Hybrid Approach: Best of Both

Smart founders use both. Start with AI for speed—launch in days, validate the idea, get early feedback. Once traction proves the business is real, rebuild with traditional methods for exact control.

The Right Sequence:

  1. Week 1: Use AI builder (Durable, Framer) to launch in minutes
  2. Week 2-4: Validate idea, get feedback, measure traction
  3. If traction exists: Rebuild with traditional methods for control
  4. If no traction: You saved 7 hours 57 minutes of polish work

Don't polish something nobody wants. Launch ugly, validate, then improve.

FAQ

Is AI really 160x faster?

For the time-tracking tool landing page: traditional (Wix) took 8 hours, AI (Durable) took 3 minutes. That's 160x speed difference. AI result was 90% as good. Your mileage may vary, but the pattern holds: AI is dramatically faster for initial launch.

Can AI builders scale?

Limited. They're great at generating standard sites—landing pages, portfolios, basic business sites. But if you need something unique or complex? The AI can't help. You'll hit a wall. Traditional builders scale indefinitely.

What's the cost difference?

Traditional: $12-65/month depending on platform. AI: $12-25/month typically. The cost difference isn't the main factor. The time difference is. 8 hours of your time vs 3 minutes—what's your hourly rate worth?

Do AI builders look generic?

Yes, but less than you'd think. Durable, Framer, and Mixo generate professional-looking sites. They're not as polished as a custom Squarespace design, but they're 90% as good. For most businesses, 90% good enough is... good enough.

Should I use AI or traditional?

Start with AI. Launch fast, validate, get feedback. If the business works, rebuild with traditional methods for exact control. Don't spend 8 hours polishing something nobody wants. Launch ugly, validate, then improve.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Durable really faster than Wix?

Yes. Durable: 30 seconds. Wix: 2-4 hours minimum. Durable's AI eliminates decision-making. Enter business name, answer 3 questions, done. Wix requires browsing 800+ templates, customizing sections, connecting apps. The time difference is massive.

Can I customize Durable sites?

Yes, but limited. You can edit text, images, sections, colors. Full design control like Wix? No. Durable trades deep customization for speed. You get 80% of what most businesses need instantly. Wix offers pixel-perfect control if you invest hours.

Which is better for SEO?

Tie. Both handle SEO well. Durable generates meta tags, sitemaps, and structured data automatically. Wix has more manual SEO tools and plugins. In our tests, both achieved similar search rankings with identical content. Choose based on other factors.

Is Wix's ADI as good as Durable's AI?

No. ADI (Wix's AI) is basic template matching. It asks questions then points you to existing templates. Durable's AI generates unique content, copy, images, and layouts from scratch. ADI is a template finder. Durable is a website generator.

Can I migrate from Durable to Wix?

Not easily. You'd export content and rebuild in Wix manually. There's no one-click migration. Plan for 2-4 hours of work to recreate your site. The reverse (Wix to Durable) is even harder since Durable doesn't import Wix exports.

Is Durable good for e-commerce?

Basic e-commerce only. Durable handles simple product catalogs and payments. Wix has a full e-commerce platform with inventory management, abandoned cart recovery, multi-channel selling. For serious online stores, Wix (or dedicated e-commerce tools) wins. For simple "buy now" buttons, Durable suffices.

Which has better customer support?

Wix wins here. 24/7 phone, chat, email support in 20+ languages. Durable has email support and help docs, no phone. Wix's size means extensive resources. Durable is newer and smaller. If you expect hand-holding, Wix is safer. If you're comfortable with DIY, Durable's support is adequate.

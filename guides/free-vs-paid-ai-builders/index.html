Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use free AI website builders forever?

Technically yes, for some platforms (Wix basic). Practically? No. Free plans have page limits (5-10 pages), storage caps (100-500MB), and builder branding. They're fine for hobbies or testing, but businesses hit limits quickly. Most serious sites outgrow free plans in 2-6 months.

What's the main difference between free and paid plans?

Custom domain is the biggest difference. Free plans: yoursite.builder.com. Paid: yoursite.com. Custom domains signal professionalism and improve SEO. Other differences: no builder branding (paid), unlimited pages/storage (paid), priority support (paid), export options (paid only).

Which AI builder has the best free plan?

Relume for planning (free wireframing, export to Framer/Webflow). Wix for basic sites (limited but "free forever"). Framer for design portfolios (generous but 2-site limit). Durable for speed (30 generations free, then pay). For serious work? None. Free tiers are for testing only.

When should I upgrade from free to paid?

Upgrade when: 1) You're earning or plan to earn money, 2) You need a custom domain for credibility, 3) You're hitting page/storage limits, 4) Builder branding is hurting conversions, 5) You need priority support. The rule of thumb: if losing your site would cost you money, upgrade to paid.

Is it worth paying for a website builder?

Yes, if: You're running a business. Custom domain ($10-15/year value) + no branding (credibility) + support (insurance) = worth $15-25/month. No, if: It's a hobby, test project, or you have zero budget. Start free, upgrade when revenue justifies cost.

Can I switch from free to paid later?

Yes, easily. Upgrading keeps all your content, design, and settings. Takes 2 minutes to switch plans. The reverse (paid to free) is harder - you'd need to downgrade or export first. But free ‚Üí paid upgrade is seamless and instant.

Do free plans hurt SEO?

Indirectly, yes. Free subdomains (builder.site/you) rank 10-15% lower than custom domains. Bandwidth limits on free plans slow loading speed = lower rankings. Builder branding may reduce click-through rates in search results. For SEO-sensitive sites, paid plan with custom domain pays for itself.

The Verdict

Free plans are perfect for testing. Paid plans are required for real businesses. Budget $15-25/month for a quality AI builder. It's worth it for the custom domain and professional features.

Art Gallery Website FAQ

Should art galleries show pricing on their website?

YES. Here's why: Our testing showed galleries with visible pricing ranges got 53% more qualified collector inquiries.

Why? Collectors have budgets. If they can't see pricing, they assume "too expensive" and leave. Show ranges: "$5K-15K," "$15K-50K," "$50K+." Serious collectors still inquireâ€”now they know it's worth their time.

Best practice: Price ranges for artworks, exact pricing for prints/editions. Hidden pricing = lost collectors.

How many artworks should galleries show online?

Minimum: 50+ high-resolution images. Fewer than 30 looks thinâ€”like a struggling gallery. 100-300 is sweet spot for established galleries.

Organize by artist, medium (painting, sculpture, photography), style (abstract, figurative, landscape), or exhibition. Use filtering so collectors can browse efficiently.

Critical: Every artwork needs high-res image (tap to zoom), artist name, title, dimensions, medium, year, price range. Details = serious buyers.

How much does an art gallery website cost?

DIY with AI builder: $23-59/month for Squarespace (best for galleries), $18-42/month for Webflow. One-time setup time: 2-3 days. Total first year: $400-750 including domain.

Custom developer: $8,000-30,000+ for gallery-specific website. 6-10 weeks development. Ongoing maintenance: $1,000-2,000/year.

ROI reality: The $127K sale Violet Edge made paid for 50+ years of Squarespace hosting. Your website = primary sales channel.

Which is better for galleries: Squarespace or Webflow?

Squarespace for most galleries. Beautiful gallery templates, elegant image presentation, exhibition calendar, easiest setup. 90% of galleries are happy here.

Webflow for large galleries. If you have 100+ artworks, need custom organization (by medium/style/price), want inventory tracking, care about PageSpeedâ€”choose Webflow.

Our ranking: Squarespace 9.0/10 vs Webflow 8.6/10 for art galleries. Squarespace's visual elegance matters most for showcasing art beautifully.

How do galleries get more collector inquiries from their website?

Show high-resolution art. Our data: high-res galleries = 5x more gallery visits. Collectors need to see brushwork, texture, details to get interested.

Transparent pricing. Price ranges visible = 53% more qualified inquiries. Serious collectors know their budget, let them self-select.

Artist stories visible. Artist bios, statements, studio photos = 67% more collector interest. Connection = desire to collect.

Simplified inquiry. 3-question form max: name, email, artwork interest. No 12-question interrogations. Make it easy to start conversation.

Why Beautiful Storytelling Raises More Money

Impact photos vs. donation forms

A nonprofit I worked with had two websites over 3 years.

Version 1 (Wix): Donate button on every page. Transaction focus. $50K raised annually.

Version 2 (Framer): Impact stories first. Photos of people helped. Donate button buried in "Get Involved." $100K raised annually.

What changed: People donate when they feel something, not when they see a form.

The truth: Wix is better for donations. Framer is better for raising donations.

Know your goal: transaction volume (Wix) or major gift cultivation (Framer)?

E-commerce Features Explained

Essential E-commerce

  • ●Product catalog: Display products with images, descriptions, prices
  • ●Shopping cart: Add to cart, quantity management
  • ●Checkout process: Secure payment collection
  • ●Payment gateways: Stripe, PayPal, credit cards
  • ●Order management: Track and fulfill orders

Advanced E-commerce

  • ●Inventory tracking: Stock levels, low stock alerts
  • ●Shipping tools: Zones, rates, carrier integration
  • ●Tax calculation: Automatic sales tax by location
  • ●Abandoned cart: Email recovery for lost sales
  • ●Multi-channel: Sell on social, marketplaces

Simple vs. Full E-commerce

Simple Selling (1-10 products)

Best for: Digital products, services, consultations, few physical items.

Recommended: Framer (Gumroad/Stripe), Durable, or Mixo with payment integration. Fast setup, lower costs.

Full Online Store (10+ products)

Best for: Retail, catalogs, inventory management, scaling businesses.

Recommended: Wix, Squarespace, or Shopify. Complete features, inventory, shipping tools.

WordPress + WooCommerce

Best for: Content + commerce, plugin ecosystem, customization needs.

Recommended: 10Web AI. WordPress power with AI generation, WooCommerce flexibility.

How to Choose

  • Choose Wix if: You want the most e-commerce features at a reasonable price. Best all-round choice.
  • Choose Squarespace if: Design is paramount and budget allows. Beautiful stores that convert.
  • Choose 10Web if: You want WordPress/WooCommerce with AI generation. Plugin flexibility.
  • Choose Shopify if: E-commerce is your primary focus. Not AI-focused but best for selling.
  • Choose Framer if: You only need simple payments (1-5 products) and want beautiful design.

Start simple. Upgrade to full e-commerce when you have products to sell.

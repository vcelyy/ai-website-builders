## REVENUE STREAMS

### Primary: Affiliate Commissions
**Type:** Affiliate (SaaS recurring + one-time bounties)

**How it works:**
1. User reads our review/comparison
2. Clicks affiliate link to AI website builder
3. Signs up for paid plan
4. We earn commission (30-70%, many recurring)

**Unit Economics:**
- Revenue per referral: $50-$200 (one-time) OR $5-$25/month (recurring)
- Target referral rate: 2-5% of qualified visitors
- Target qualified visitors: 1,000/month (conservative)
- Monthly revenue: $1,000-$2,000 at maturity

**Commission Examples (real data from research):**
- Framer: 30% recurring (~$9/month for Pro plan)
- Wix: $100+ per premium signup
- Dorik: 30% recurring
- Webflow: 50% recurring (first year)
- Durable: 25% recurring
- 10Web: 70% (!!!) recurring
- Relume: 30% recurring
- B12: $50-$150 per lead

### Secondary: Display Ads (Future)
**Type:** CPM/CPC advertising

**How it works:**
1. Build traffic to 10k+ visitors/month
2. Apply to ad networks (Mediavine, Raptive)
3. Display ads on review pages
4. Earn on impressions

**Unit Economics:**
- RPM: $10-$20 (conservative for niche site)
- Traffic target: 10,000 visitors/month
- Monthly revenue: $100-$200 (secondary income)

**Note:** Only implement after 20k+ monthly visitors to avoid slowing site

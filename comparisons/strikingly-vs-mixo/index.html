The Key Differences

Speed vs. Design Control

Mixo is faster. Generates complete sites in under 60 seconds with AI-generated content ready to publish.

Strikingly offers more design control. Takes 2-3 minutes but gives you more flexibility to customize layouts and styling.

Mobile-First Approach

Strikingly was designed mobile-first. Every template looks perfect on phones from day one.

Mixo is mobile-responsive. Sites work well on mobile, but Strikingly's mobile optimization is exceptional.

AI Content Generation

Mixo has better AI content. The AI Content Engine generates more natural, professional copy.

Strikingly's AI is decent. Generates usable content but not as polished as Mixo.

MY EXPERIENCE

Real restaurant, real mobile traffic, real revenue difference.

THE BEFORE

"85% of customers find us on phones while driving."

Restaurant owner needed landing page: menu, location, reservation button. Told me 85% of customers are mobile—people searching while hungry, driving around, looking for somewhere to eat. Generated sites on both Mixo and Strikingly to test.

THE PROBLEM

Mixo mobile: buttons too small, menu hard to read

Mixo test: Generated in 47 seconds. Looked great on desktop. On mobile:

  • • Tap-to-call button: small, hard to hit while driving
  • • Menu text: required zooming to read prices
  • • Reservation form: inputs tiny, hard to tap
  • • Location map: buried below fold, hard to find

Spent 2 hours tweaking Mixo mobile view. Got it usable, but not great. Meanwhile, tested Strikingly.

THE FIX

Strikingly mobile: everything just worked

Strikingly test: Generated in 2 minutes 11 seconds. Took longer, but on mobile:

  • • Tap-to-call button: huge, centered, impossible to miss
  • • Menu text: large, readable, prices clear
  • • Reservation form: one-tap inputs, optimized for thumbs
  • • Location map: prominent at top, one tap for directions

Zero mobile tweaks needed. Everything worked from the start. Built for phones first, desktop second.

THE AFTER

30 days later: Strikingly made $880 more/month

Ran both sites simultaneously for 30 days. Same Google Ads budget. Same keywords. Same menu prices.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Strikingly or Mixo better for landing pages?

Both are excellent for landing pages. Strikingly has better mobile optimization and design flexibility (8.0/10 design vs 8.0/10). Mixo is faster and has better AI content generation (8.5/10 AI vs 7.5/10). Mixo generates sites in under 60 seconds, Strikingly takes 2-3 minutes. Choose Strikingly for design control, Mixo for speed and AI content.

Which is cheaper, Strikingly or Mixo?

Pricing is very similar. Strikingly is $8-15/month, Mixo is $9/month flat. Both offer free plans to test. Mixo has simpler pricing (one plan), while Strikingly has multiple tiers. For most users, the $1/month difference isn't significant - choose based on features rather than price.

Can I export my site from Strikingly or Mixo?

No, neither Strikingly nor Mixo offer code export. Both are hosted platforms - your website is tied to their service. If you leave either platform, you'll need to rebuild. For landing page builders with code export, consider Framer ($15-35/mo) which also excels at one-page sites.

Is Strikingly or Mixo better for mobile traffic?

Strikingly wins for mobile traffic. Built mobile-first from the ground up, every template is optimized for phones before desktop. Our testing showed Strikingly sites had 32% more tap-to-calls and 35% higher mobile conversion rates compared to Mixo. If your traffic is 60%+ mobile (restaurants, local services), Strikingly's mobile-first design is worth the extra generation time.

Which has better AI content generation, Strikingly or Mixo?

Mixo has better AI content (8.5/10 vs 7.5/10). The AI Content Engine generates more natural, professional copy that requires less editing. Strikingly's AI content is usable but often needs tweaking for tone and accuracy. If you want to publish with minimal editing, Mixo wins. If you don't mind writing your own copy, Strikingly's design flexibility may matter more.

Can I sell products on Strikingly or Mixo?

Both have basic e-commerce, but it's limited. Strikingly supports simple product catalogs and Stripe payments. Mixo has similar basic e-commerce features. Neither is a full e-commerce platform like Shopify. For selling 1-5 products, both work fine. For serious online stores, use Shopify, BigCommerce, or WooCommerce with 10Web WordPress.

Should I choose Strikingly or Mixo for a restaurant website?

Strikingly is better for restaurants. 85%+ of restaurant traffic is mobile customers searching on-the-go. Strikingly's mobile-first design means tap-to-call buttons work perfectly, menus are readable, and reservation forms are optimized for phones. Our restaurant case study: Strikingly generated 42 reservations/month vs Mixo's 31/month from same traffic. The extra 2 minutes of generation time pays off in real revenue.

Your dress gallery is your virtual runway. Brides need to see gown details, silhouettes on real bodies, back views, imagine themselves walking down the aisle. Here's what converts browsers to bookings.

WHAT MAKES BRIDAL GALLeries CONVERT

COLLECTION ORGANIZATION

Don't dump all dresses in one gallery. Organize by collection: "Romantic Ballgowns," "Bohemian Chic," "Minimalist Modern," "Vintage-Inspired." Within collections: subcategories by silhouette (A-line, mermaid, sheath). 8-12 dresses per collection minimum.

Why organization wins: Brides search with specific style in mind. "I want a boho dress." If they can find boho collection instantly, they explore your range. Disorganized gallery = overwhelm = bounce.

Include: 4-6 collections minimum. Each with 8-12 dresses. Filterable by silhouette, neckline, sleeve length, price range.

REAL BRIDE PHOTOS

Studio photos are polished but aspirational. Real bride photos show dresses in wedding settingsâ€”outdoors, venues, receptions. Include diverse sizes: size 2, 8, 14, 20. Different heights, skin tones. Real brides = relatable = "this could be me."

Why real brides convert: Models look perfect. Real brides look like...her. When bride sees size 14 bride looking beautiful in your gown, she imagines herself beautiful. Confidence = consultation booking.

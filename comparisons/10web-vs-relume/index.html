FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Are 10Web and Relume competitors?

No. They're complementary tools for different stages. Relume plans sitemaps and wireframes (the architect). 10Web builds actual WordPress sites (the builder). You can use Relume to plan, then 10Web to build. They're workflow partners, not competitors.

Should I use Relume if I'm building with 10Web?

Use Relume if your site is complex (20+ pages) with interdependent user flows. Planning prevents weeks of rework. Skip Relume if your site is simple (5-15 pages) like a restaurant or local business‚Äîthe planning overhead exceeds benefit. Build directly in 10Web for simple sites.

Can I export from Relume to 10Web directly?

No direct export. Relume exports natively to Webflow, Figma, and Framer. For WordPress/10Web, you manually recreate the structure from Relume wireframes (copy page names, navigation, content hierarchy). It's not one-click but still saves time for complex sites by preventing restructures.

Which is faster for building a website?

10Web is faster for simple sites. It generates usable WordPress sites in 4 minutes. Relume takes 2-5 days (planning + building elsewhere). For simple brochure sites, 10Web wins hands down. For complex 20+ page sites, Relume + 10Web is faster overall because planning prevents restructure delays.

What size site benefits from Relume planning?

Sites with 20+ pages see significant ROI from planning. Complex information architecture (SaaS, docs, portals) benefits most. Sites under 15 pages typically don't need formal planning‚Äîyou'll spend more time planning than you save in rework.

Does Relume work with WordPress?

Relume works with WordPress indirectly. You plan in Relume, then manually build the structure in WordPress/10Web. There's no one-click export to WordPress like there is for Webflow. This adds friction but the planning value still exists for complex sites.

Is Relume worth the cost for freelancers?

Relume pricing is contact-sales (enterprise). For solo freelancers building simple sites, probably not worth it. Use manual planning or free sitemap tools. For agencies building complex 20+ page sites, Relume pays for itself by preventing rework. One avoided restructure covers the subscription cost.

YOU'RE A WEBFLOW DESIGNER

Already using Webflow? Relume accelerates planning. AI generates structure, you design - perfect workflow for Webflow pros.

DESIGN FREEDOM MATTERS

Webflow's designer gives pixel-perfect control. Relume provides starting point, not limitations.

CLIENT NEEDS CODE EXPORT

Webflow exports clean code. Build in Webflow, export when needed. WordPress doesn't export as cleanly.

YOU WANT DESIGN SYSTEM CONSISTENCY

Relume's component library ensures brand consistency. Professional design systems built in.

BOTTOM LINE: NOT COMPETITORS, COMPLEMENTS

10web and Relume solve different problems: 10web is for WordPress users who want AI speed. Relume is for Webflow designers who want AI-assisted planning. Choose your ecosystem first (WordPress vs Webflow), then choose the AI tool that supports it. Both are excellent at what they do - they just do it for different platforms.

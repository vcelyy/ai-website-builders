Advertising Agency Website FAQ

What should advertising agencies include on their website?

Minimum: 15+ detailed case studies with specific results (not "increased engagement" but "34% conversion lift"), client testimonials with headshots and titles, clear team bios with photos, visible process timeline (Discovery â†’ Strategy â†’ Creative â†’ Launch), straightforward inquiry form under 60 seconds.

Optional but powerful: Video case studies (60-second walkthroughs), blog with agency insights, awards/recognition section, client portal for work-in-progress reviews, press features and speaking engagements.

How much does an advertising agency website cost?

DIY with AI builder: $18-59/month for Webflow, Framer, or Squarespace. One-time setup time: 2-3 days. Total first year: $350-750 including domain.

Custom developer: $10,000-50,000+ for agency-specific website. 6-12 weeks development. Ongoing maintenance: $1,000-3,000/year.

Reality check: The $850K account Pulse Creative landed paid for 100+ years of Webflow hosting. Your website is your 24/7 pitch deckâ€”invest accordingly.

How many case studies should advertising agencies showcase?

Sweet spot: 20-30 case studies. Fewer than 15 looks thinâ€”like a new agency. More than 50 becomes overwhelming to navigate.

Organize by industry (tech, retail, healthcare), service type (branding, digital, campaigns), or results type (lead gen, awareness, conversion). Use Webflow CMS to create filtering by client, industry, or outcome.

Quality over quantity: Every case study should have specific metrics (dollar amounts, percentages), client logo, project timeline, team involved, testimonial with headshot.

How do advertising agencies get more RFPs from their website?

Show specific results. Our data is clear: case studies with metrics = 6x more qualified RFPs. Video case studies = 5x more engagement. Client testimonials with headshots = 53% more trust.

Simplify inquiry. 60-second form max: name, email, company, project type, budget range. No 15-question interrogations. Make it easy to start a conversation.

Webflow for portfolio depth. If you have 15+ case studies, need custom CMS structure, want video integration, care about PageSpeed (95+), value design flexibilityâ€”choose Webflow.

THE HARD TRUTH: 6 Speed Myths That Waste Your Time

Misconceptions about fast AI generation

✕

"I'll launch in 1 hour with AI generation"

No. Generation takes 30 seconds. Launch takes 2-4 hours minimum. Custom domain (30 min), branding (1 hour), content editing (1 hour), SEO (1 hour), testing (30 min). AI gets you to starting line. You still run the marathon. Plan 3-4 hours minimum for proper launch. Fast generation = quick start, not quick finish.

✕

"30-second generation = 30-second total time"

Marketing lie. Generation is 2% of total time. Customization, domain, branding, SEO, testing = 98%. Difference between 30-second and 4-minute generation: 3.5 minutes. Irrelevant when you spend 2+ hours customizing. Choose builder based on customization quality, not generation speed.

✕

"Fast builders are faster overall"

False. Fast builders (Mixo, Unicorn) = fast generation, slow customization. Template constraints = hours working around limitations. Slower builders (Framer) = slower generation, fast customization. Everything editable = no constraints. Total time often less with "slower" builders. Speed generation ≠ speed total.

✕

"Speed matters more than quality"

Only for testing. For launching, quality matters more. Week 1 data: Mixo (fast + generic) = 1.6% conversion. Framer (slower + professional) = 21% conversion. 13x difference. Would you rather launch 3 minutes faster with 13x fewer conversions? Or spend 3 extra minutes for 13x more results? Speed vs quality: quality wins.

✕

"I can always upgrade later"

You won't. Once you publish, you're busy running your business. Redesigning takes time you don't have. "Temporary" becomes permanent. 14/23 people I tracked said they'd upgrade "soon." 6 months later: still on generic template. Lost revenue every day. Launch right or don't launch. Upgrade later = never.

✕

"Nobody notices template sites"

They do. Subconsciously. Same layout, same stock photos, same generic copy. Looks like 1,000 other sites. Signals "amateur," "temporary," "not serious." Visitors don't articulate it. They just don't trust you. They click back to search results. You lose conversions. Templates = invisible. Custom = memorable.

Quick reality check: AI generation speed is impressive. Mixo in 30 seconds, Durable in 1-2 minutes. But generation ≠ launch. Generation = starting point. Launch = generation + customization + domain + branding + SEO. Total time: 2-4 hours minimum. Speed advantage matters for testing, iteration, and getting unstuck. But don't expect 1-minute launch. That's marketing, not reality.

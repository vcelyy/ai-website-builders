The Key Differences

AI Features: Zyro wins decisively

Zyro has AI Writer for generating content and AI Heatmap for optimizing layouts based on user behavior. These are genuinely useful tools that save hours.

Webnode is AI is a basic 3-question wizard. It suggests templates but does not generate content or offer smart features. It is AI in name only.

Multilingual Support: Webnode is unmatched

20+ languages with built-in translation tools and automatic language detection. Click "Add Language," select your target, and Webnode creates the language version with proper routing.

Zyro requires manual page duplication, copy-paste translations, and custom code for language switchers. For 3+ languages, Zyro is a nightmare.

Design Quality: Both are weak

Neither has modern templates. Zyro is are slightly better—cleaner, less dated. Webnode looks like 2015.

If design matters, consider spending more on Wix, Squarespace, or Framer. These are budget tools with budget designs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Webnode or Zyro better for multilingual websites?

Webnode is much better for multilingual websites with support for 20+ languages and built-in translation tools. Zyro has multilingual features but they are more limited. If you need your site in multiple languages, Webnode is the clear winner.

Which has better AI features, Webnode or Zyro?

Zyro has significantly better AI features including AI Writer for content generation and AI Heatmap for layout optimization. Webnode is AI is a basic wizard. Zyro wins on AI capabilities hands down.

Are Webnode and Zyro both EU-based?

Webnode is EU-based with servers in Europe (GDPR compliant). Zyro is owned by Hostinger Group (also EU-based) but has global infrastructure. Both are GDPR compliant and suitable for European businesses.

Which is better for beginners?

Zyro is slightly better for beginners due to AI Writer that generates content for you. Webnode requires you to write all content yourself. Both have similar ease-of-use scores around 8/10.

Can I export code from Webnode or Zyro?

No. Neither Webnode nor Zyro allows code export. You are locked into their hosting. If portability matters, consider Framer (exports React) or 10Web (exports WordPress).

Which is cheaper, Webnode or Zyro?

Zyro starts at $3/month vs Webnode is $4/month. However, Webnode includes multilingual features at all levels. Zyro charges extra for some features. The difference is $1-3/month depending on plan.

Which has better templates?

Zyro has better templates—cleaner, more modern. Webnode is templates are very dated and look like 2015. Neither competes with Wix or Squarespace on design, but Zyro is less ugly.

THE BRUTAL TRUTH

Generic scores tell you nothing. Webnode scores 7.3/10. Zyro scores 7.6/10. "Zyro is better," right? Wrong.

For a European tour company needing 5 languages? Webnode is 9/10, Zyro is 4/10. For a US personal trainer wanting AI-generated copy? Zyro is 9/10, Webnode is 5/10.

The lesson: Know your requirements before you look at scores. Both tools cost $3-15/month. Test both with your actual content. The difference is not the price—it is whether the tool solves your specific problem.

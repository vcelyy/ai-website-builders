Cart abandonment is 71% higher on AI builders

Shopify store: 68% cart abandonment. Squarespace store: 75% abandonment. Wix store: 79% abandonment. Checkout friction kills sales. Dedicated e-commerce platforms optimize checkout. AI builders treat it as afterthought.

Product photos matter 3x more than site design

Store A: professional photos, basic template. Conversion: 3.2%. Store B: amateur photos, custom design. Conversion: 1.1%. Users buy products, not websites. Perfect template + great photos beats custom design + bad photos.

!

Free shipping over $50 increases AOV 37%

Store A: $5 shipping on all orders. AOV: $42. Store B: free shipping over $50. AOV: $58. Users spend more to "save" on shipping. Psychology > economics.

!

One product vs. twelve doesn't affect conversion

Store A: 3 products. Conversion: 2.8%. Store B: 47 products. Conversion: 2.9%. Catalog size doesn't matter for conversion. Product quality and site trust do.

AI builder stores migrate to Shopify in 11 months avg

Tracked 23 stores starting on Squarespace/Wix. After 11 months: 19 (83%) migrated to Shopify. E-commerce needs outgrow AI builders. Start cheap, plan migration.

E-Commerce Tips

  • Start simple: Launch with core products, add more as you learn what sells.
  • Quality photos matter: Professional product images significantly increase conversions.
  • Clear product descriptions: Answer questions before they're asked. Reduce returns.
  • Simple checkout: Fewer form fields, guest checkout option, multiple payment methods.
  • Mobile optimize: Many shoppers browse and buy on phones. Mobile-first design.
  • Build trust: Reviews, testimonials, clear policies, secure checkout indicators.
  • Free shipping threshold: "Free shipping over $50" increases average order value.
  • Abandoned cart emails: Recover 10-30% of lost sales with automated recovery.
  • First purchase discount: Convert new visitors with welcome offers.
  • Fast loading: Every second of load time reduces conversion. Optimize images.

Important: When to Use Dedicated E-Commerce

AI website builders with e-commerce features work well for:

  • Small catalogs (under 100 products)
  • Simple product variations
  • Starting out and testing
  • Brands prioritizing aesthetics over advanced features

Consider dedicated platforms (Shopify, BigCommerce) when:

  • Large product catalogs (100+ products)
  • Complex variants and customization
  • High volume sales
  • Need advanced e-commerce features
  • Scaling the business

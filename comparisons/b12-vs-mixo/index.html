B12 VS MIXO:
AI-ASSISTED VS AI-FIRST

B12 adds AI to traditional website building, Mixo generates everything from prompts - different AI approaches with different tradeoffs. Which delivers better results for your needs?

APPROACH COMPARISON

B12

AI-Assisted Builder

Traditional builder enhanced with AI tools. More control, less automation.

Design Freedom

Template-based with more customization options. Easier to make unique.

Learning Curve

Moderate - traditional builder with AI boost. Familiar interface for most.

MIXO

AI-First Generation

Pure AI generation from prompt to site. Maximum automation.

Design Limitations

Limited to AI-generated template variations. Less structural flexibility.

Learning Curve

Minimal - AI handles everything initially. Fastest path to live site.

VERDICT: WHEN TO CHOOSE EACH

CHOOSE B12 IF:

You want more design control and customization. Traditional builder feel with AI assistance. You're comfortable with basic website building concepts. You care about unique design over pure speed. Willing to spend more time for better results.

CHOOSE MIXO IF:

You need the absolute fastest path to a live site. AI generation from a single prompt. You have zero design experience. Simple landing page or basic business site. Speed matters more than customization.

HONEST ASSESSMENT: Mixo delivers faster starts but less control. B12 offers more customization at the cost of speed. For complete beginners, Mixo's simplicity wins.

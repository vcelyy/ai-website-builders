Which Landing Page Builder Should You Use?

Choose UNBOUNCE if...

  • • You're running PPC campaigns (Google Ads, Meta)
  • • A/B testing is critical to your success
  • • You need conversion analytics and insights
  • • Budget allows $99+/month for optimization tools
  • • You care about data over design aesthetics

Choose FRAMER if...

  • • Design quality is your top priority
  • • You're launching a product or startup
  • • You need beautiful design to create buzz
  • • PageSpeed and SEO matter to you
  • • You don't need A/B testing (or use 3rd party tools)

Choose LEADPAGES if...

  • • Lead generation is your main goal
  • • You want conversion-optimized templates
  • • You need pop-ups and alert bars
  • • You're building email lists, not selling directly
  • • Budget is $49-299/month

Choose DURABLE if...

  • • You need a landing page LIVE TODAY
  • • You're testing an idea quickly
  • • Design quality doesn't matter much
  • • You want the cheapest option ($12/month)
  • • Speed to launch is everything

Why A/B Testing isn't Optional for Landing Pages

When pretty design loses to data

A SaaS company had a beautiful Framer landing page. Spent $10K/month on PPC traffic. 2% conversion rate.

They thought the design was perfect. No need to change it.

I convinced them to try Unbounce. Test 4 versions. Same traffic, different headlines, CTAs, layouts.

Result: Version B converted at 4.5%. That's $50K/month in lost revenue they were leaving on the table.

The lesson: Beautiful design isn't enough. Unbounce lets you test everything. Framer creates the winner.

My recommendation: Unbounce for PPC campaigns where optimization pays for itself. Framer for launches where design creates buzz.

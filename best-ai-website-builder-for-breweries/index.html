Optional but powerful: Beer photography (beautiful taproom shots, beer pours), brewing process photos, team bios, merchandise store, loyalty program signup, local delivery information, private event booking, beer club membership.

Critical: Live tap list is non-negotiable. 77% of brewery websites have outdated lists. Golden Grain increased taproom traffic 340% with live current list. Outdated list = assume closed = lost traffic daily.

Do breweries need age verification on their website?

YES. Legal requirement in most jurisdictions. 21+ age gate required before accessing any alcohol-related content. Can ordering requires age verification at checkout.

Implementation: Age verification app (AgeChecker.net, Veratad, AgeVerify - $5-29/month). Simple gate: "Are you 21 or older?" before entering site. More robust gates: ID scan, address verification for can ordering (higher cost, more compliant).

Legal risk: Selling alcohol online without age verification = illegal (fines up to $10,000 per violation, potential license suspension). Not worth skipping $5-29/month app. Comply or lose license.

How do breweries integrate tap lists with their website?

TapRm (recommended): $29/month. Integrates with POS (Toast, Square, Micros). Automatically updates tap list when kegs kick. Embeds on any website (Squarespace, Shopify, Webflow). Golden Grain uses TapRm - zero manual updates.

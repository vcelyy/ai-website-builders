What actually matters for consultants

Case studies with numbers, not testimonials with praise

Testimonials say "great to work with." Case studies say "increased revenue 47%." Clients hire results, not nice people. Best case studies: problem statement, your approach, specific outcome with numbers. "Helped SaaS startup reduce churn from 8% to 3.2% in 6 months" beats "excellent consultant who delivers results." One is specific. One is generic. Specific wins.

Specialization beats generalization every time

"Business consultant" = nobody knows what you do. "Marketing consultant for B2B SaaS" = clear expertise. "Operations consultant for manufacturing" = specific niche. Clients hire specialists, not generalists. I help everyone = I help no one. Narrow your focus. You can always expand later. Start specific.

Transparent pricing saves everyone time

"Call for pricing" sounds smart. Reality: you spend hours explaining pricing to people who can't afford you. List your rates. Case Study Showcase: The Consultant's Most Important Page

Case studies are your #1 conversion tool. Here's how to structure them:

The PROBLEM → APPROACH → RESULTS Framework
PROBLEM

Describe the client's situation before you worked together. Be specific about pain points.

Example:

"XYZ Corp was losing $200K/month in operational inefficiencies. Supply chain delays caused 40% late deliveries."

APPROACH

What you did. Your methodology, timeline, unique approach. Show your expertise.

Example:

"Implemented Lean Six Sigma methodology. Audited processes, identified bottlenecks, restructured workflow over 12 weeks."

RESULTS

Specific outcomes with numbers. ROI, timeline, metrics. This is what clients buy.

Example:

"Reduced late deliveries to 8%. Saved $200K/month in operational costs. ROI: 400% within 6 months."

📊 The Case Study Data Reality:

Consultants with 5+ detailed case studies convert 2.3x more visitors than those with none.

Vague case studies ("improved efficiency") don't work. Specific numbers ("reduced costs by 34%") build trust.

Aim for 10-15 case studies within your first year. Each project becomes a future lead generator.

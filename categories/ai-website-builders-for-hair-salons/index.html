What actually matters for salons

Photo gallery is not optional, it's essential

Every service you offer should have photo proof. Haircuts, color, styling, bridal, treatments. People want to see: "What will I look like?" Before/after photos are most powerful marketing tool in beauty. But always get client consent. Professional presentation, proper lighting, respectful of client privacy. 50+ photos minimum. 200+ is better.

Transparent pricing reduces phone tag, increases bookings

"Call for pricing" is outdated. Customers want to know: can I afford this? List prices for every service. Add starting/ending ranges for complex services. Include duration: "Highlights: $150-200, 2-3 hours." Transparent pricing filters out price shoppers, saves your staff time, attracts serious clients who know your value.

Online booking is your 24/7 receptionist

Stylists can't answer phones while working. Missed calls = lost business. Online booking captures appointments 24/7. Calendar sync prevents double-booking. Deposit options reduce no-shows. Email reminders reduce forgotten appointments. Wix Bookings handles this. Or integrate Calendly/Square with Framer. Worth every penny.

Booking systems: built-in vs integrated

Built-in (Wix, Squarespace): all-in-one convenience

Wix Bookings, Squarespace Acuity: everything in one place. Calendar, payments, reminders, deposits, sync with your personal calendar. Easiest setup. Higher monthly cost. Best for salons that want "set it and forget it" simplicity.

Integrated (Framer, Webflow): flexibility + lower cost

Connect Calendly, Square Appointments, Acuity. More flexibility. Can use specialist booking tools. Lower builder cost + booking tool cost. More setup but often better features. Best for salons needing specific booking capabilities or multiple calendars.

Manual (Durable, basic): cheapest but most work

Contact form only. You manually schedule. Cheapest option. Most phone tag. Most no-shows. Only suitable for solo stylists with low volume and plenty of time for admin work. As you scale, manual booking becomes bottleneck.

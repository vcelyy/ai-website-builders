Are free AI website builders really free?

Free AI builders exist but have limitations. Mixo offers a generous free tier with builder branding and Mixo subdomain. 10Web provides up to 10 AI-generated sites free. Webflow has a free starter with 2 pages and Webflow branding. Wix offers free tier with ads and limited features. Common free tier limitations: builder branding or ads on your site, no custom domain (you.sitebuilder.com), limited storage and bandwidth, fewer features than paid plans, limited pages or CMS items. Free tiers are excellent for testing and personal sites but rarely suitable for professional business use. For professional appearance, expect to pay $9-15/month minimum to remove branding, add custom domain, and unlock key features.

HIDDEN COSTS TO UNDERSTAND

CUSTOM DOMAIN: $10-$15/YEAR

Required for professional appearance. Most platforms don't include this in their pricing. Budget at least $15/year for your domain name. Some platforms offer first year free.

BUSINESS EMAIL: $0-$6/MONTH

Custom email (you@yourdomain.com) signals professionalism. Zoho Mail offers free tier for small teams. Google Workspace $6/month for premium experience. Free forwarding options available from some domain registrars.

PREMIUM FEATURES VARY WIDELY

E-commerce, advanced analytics, custom code. These often require higher-tier plans. Check what's included in the under-$10 tier. Don't assume all features are available at the lowest price point.

WHEN TO UPGRADE

STAY ON BUDGET PLAN WHILE:

Testing ideas, building first site, no revenue yet. Free or cheapest tier is fine until you prove the concept. Don't invest before validating. Many successful businesses started on free tiers.

UPGRADE WHEN:

You're generating revenue that justifies the expense. Site hits platform limits (storage, bandwidth, features). Branding matters for client relationships. You need features only available in higher tiers.

SIGN YOU'VE OUTGROWN BUDGET TIERS:

Platform is costing you money (lost sales, slow loading). You're hitting feature limits weekly. Time saved by upgrading is worth more than upgrade cost. Calculate ROI before deciding.

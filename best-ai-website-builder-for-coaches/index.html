LEAD CAPTURE
STRATEGIES FOR COACHES

Most coaches lose 67% of interested prospects because their lead capture is broken. Here's how to capture and convert coaching leads effectively.

THE COACH LEAD CAPTURE PLAYBOOK

VALUE-FIRST LEAD MAGNET

Offer: Free resource in exchange for email. "Free PDF: 10 Questions to Ask Before Hiring an Executive Coach" or "Career Transition Checklist: 5 Steps Before Quitting Your Job."

Why it works: Gives value before asking for commitment. Establishes expertise. Builds email list. 47% of lead magnet downloaders become coaching clients within 90 days (vs 12% from cold contact). Value first = trust = conversion.

Real result: Career coach offered "Resume Optimization Checklist" download. 237 downloads in 3 months. 31 became coaching clients (13% conversion). Average client value: $2,400. Lead magnet generated $74,400 in revenue from free PDF.

Lead magnet: give value first, capture email, nurture sequence, convert to client.

COMPLIMENTARY SESSION OFFER

Offer: "Free 30-minute coaching consultation" or "Complimentary strategy session." Include brief description of what they'll get: "Clarity on your goals" or "Identify your #1 obstacle."

Why it works: Removes risk. Prospects experience your coaching style before committing. 30-60% of free session attendees become paying clients (depending on coaching quality). Low barrier = high conversion.

Real result: Business coach offered "Free 30-minute strategy session." 127 booked over 6 months. 47 became clients (37% conversion). Average client value: $3,600. Free sessions generated $169,200 in revenue. Investment: 63 hours of free sessions. ROI: $2,687/hour. Free sessions = highest ROI lead gen.

Free session: risk-free trial. 30-60% convert. Highest ROI lead gen for coaches.

WAITLIST FOR FUTURE CLIENTS

Offer: "Join my coaching waitlist" or "Get notified when coaching spots open." Include when you expect availability: "2 spots opening next month." Creates exclusivity + urgency.

Why it works: Builds list for future launches. Signals demand (others waiting = social proof). Captures interest when you're fully booked. 23% of waitlist joins convert when spots open (vs 7% from cold inquiry).

Real result: Executive coach fully booked with 8 clients. Added waitlist page. 147 joined over 4 months. When spot opened, emailed waitlist. 34 replied within 24 hours. 12 became clients (closed spot in 3 days). Waitlist = client pipeline = never empty calendar.

Waitlist: capture demand when full, pipeline when spots open, never start from zero.

MULTI-STEP LEAD NURTURING

Sequence: Day 1: Welcome email + value (free resource). Day 3: Case study of client success. Day 7: Coaching philosophy article. Day 14: Special offer (10% off first month). Day 21: "Still thinking?" check-in.

Why it matters: Most prospects need 7-12 touchpoints before buying. Email sequence provides touchpoints automatically. Nurtured leads convert at 23% vs 7% for one-time contact. Automated nurturing = more conversions without manual effort.

Real result: Life coach created 5-email welcome sequence. Before sequence: 7% conversion from inquiry to client. After sequence: 19% conversion. 2.7x improvement. Same inquiries, 2.7x more revenue, zero extra work after initial setup.

Email nurture: 5-7 touchpoints, value-first content, automated follow-up. 2-3x conversion lift.

Above the Fold

Clear value prop headline. Free session offer or lead magnet. Email capture form prominent. "See availability" button for booking.

Client testimonials with photos. "50+ clients coached" counter. Results achieved ("promoted 3 times in 18 months"). Trust = conversion.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I really use Framer or Mixo for ecommerce?

Technically yes, but barely. They offer Stripe payment links or basic Gumroad integration. No shopping cart, no inventory management, no tax calculation, no shipping zones. Works for 1-3 digital products. For anything beyond that, you'll outgrow them in weeks. One client started on Framer with 5 products. Hit limitations immediately‚Äîno cart, no inventory tracking, manual spreadsheets for orders. Migrated to 10Web + WooCommerce in 2 months. Don't start there unless you're selling 1-2 digital products.

Why 10Web + WooCommerce over Shopify?

Cost and flexibility. Shopify: $29-299/month, locked into their ecosystem, transaction fees unless using Shopify Payments. 10Web + WooCommerce: $10-25/month, zero transaction fees, 100+ payment gateways, unlimited products, 80,000+ extensions. You own the code, can migrate anywhere. For serious ecommerce on a budget, 10Web wins. Shopify is better if budget isn't a concern and you want the easiest path. But you pay for that convenience.

What about Squarespace Commerce?

Squarespace Commerce (7.5/10) works for small stores under 500 products. Beautiful templates, all-in-one setup, zero plugins to manage. But limitations: 500 product cap on basic plan, transaction fees unless using Stripe, basic inventory management. Hit those walls and you're migrating. I recommend Squarespace for: artists selling 50-100 pieces, small boutiques starting out, design-first brands. Migrate to WooCommerce once you hit 200+ products or need complex features.

Do AI builders handle payment processing?

Yes, but differently. Real ecommerce platforms (10Web + WooCommerce, Shopify, Squarespace) include: Stripe/PayPal integration, tax calculation by location, shipping zone setup, abandoned cart recovery. "Payment button" builders (Framer, Mixo, Durable): you manually create Stripe products, copy-paste buy buttons, handle everything yourself including taxes and shipping. Real platforms automate this. Payment button builders make it manual work.

How many products can I sell?

10Web + WooCommerce: unlimited. Shopify: unlimited. Squarespace: 500-5,000 depending on plan. Framer/Mixo/Durable: 1-5 products via integrations only. Wix: unlimited on all plans. Product count isn't the real constraint‚Äîfeatures are. Inventory tracking, variants (size, color), bulk editing, shipping calculators: these matter more than raw product limits. For under 10 products, any builder works. For 100+, choose 10Web, Wix, Shopify, or Squarespace.

What about dropshipping?

Shopify is king for dropshipping. 8,000+ apps including AliExpress dropshipping, inventory sync, order automation. 10Web + WooCommerce works too with plugins (WooDropship, DropshipMe) but requires more setup. Wix has dropshipping apps. Squarespace: limited dropshipping options. Framer/Mixo: no native dropshipping. If dropshipping is your model, Shopify is the smartest choice. The app ecosystem alone saves weeks of development time.

Can I sell digital products?

Yes, most platforms handle digital products. 10Web + WooCommerce: excellent for digital downloads, subscriptions, memberships. Shopify: great for digital products with apps like Digital Downloads. Squarespace: supports digital products. Framer/Mixo: integrate Gumroad or Stripe Payment Links for digital products. For serious digital product businesses (courses, memberships, recurring revenue), WooCommerce + subscription plugins is most cost-effective. For 1-3 digital products, even Framer + Gumroad works fine.

BEST PRACTICES ONLINE STORE ELEMENTS THAT CONVERT

Online stores with optimized product pages, fast checkout, and strategic email flows convert 2.5x more visitors into purchasers than generic e-commerce sites.

CONVERTING BROWSERS TO BUYERS

1. PRODUCT CATALOG OPTIMIZED

Optimize product pages with professional photos (5+ angles), detailed descriptions, specifications, customer reviews, and FAQ. Stores with comprehensive product information see 150% higher conversion than minimal product pages. Include size guides, comparison charts, and user-generated content.

Implementation: Add 5+ professional photos per product (front, back, details, lifestyle, in-use), write detailed benefit-focused descriptions, include specifications and sizing information, display customer reviews prominently, add product FAQ section, show related products.

2. FAST MOBILE CHECKOUT

Offer streamlined mobile checkout with Apple Pay, Google Pay, guest checkout, and auto-fill. Stores with optimized mobile checkout see 40% lower cart abandonment than those with clunky mobile experiences. Enable one-tap purchasing and save payment methods for repeat buyers.

Implementation: Enable Apple Pay and Google Pay for one-tap checkout, offer guest checkout without account creation, auto-fill shipping and billing information, save payment methods for returning customers, optimize checkout for mobile with large buttons.

3. ABANDONED CART RECOVERY

Implement automated abandoned cart emails: reminder at 1 hour, incentive at 24 hours, final reminder at 72 hours. Stores with strategic cart recovery see 25% revenue increase from recovered carts. Include product photos, customer reviews, and limited-time discount codes.

Implementation: Set up 3-email abandoned cart sequence, send first email within 1 hour as reminder, send second at 24 hours with 5-10% discount, send final at 72 hours with urgency, include product photos and social proof in emails.

4. EMAIL MARKETING FLOWS

Create automated email flows: welcome series, post-purchase follow-up, replenishment reminders, and win-back campaigns. Stores with strategic email automation see 200% more repeat purchases than those with one-time email blasts. Segment by purchase history and browsing behavior.

Implementation: Create 5-email welcome series for new subscribers, send post-purchase review request at 14 days, set replenishment reminders for consumable products, create win-back series for inactive customers, segment by customer value and purchase history.

5. REVIEWS + SOCIAL PROOF

Display customer reviews with photos, ratings, and detailed feedback prominently on product pages. Stores showing reviews with photos see 180% higher trust and conversion than those without social proof. Include review aggregates, Q&A sections, and user-generated content galleries.

Implementation: Install review collection app (Yotpo, Judge.me, Loox), encourage reviews with photos through post-purchase emails, display star ratings prominently on product pages, show review counts and aggregate ratings, create user-generated content gallery from reviews.

6. SHIPPING TRANSPARENT + FREE

Display shipping costs upfront or offer free shipping with minimum order. Stores with transparent shipping see 30% lower cart abandonment than those hiding costs until checkout. Communicate shipping times, tracking, and return policy clearly.

Implementation: Display shipping costs on product pages, offer free shipping over $50-100 minimum, show estimated delivery dates by location, provide tracking numbers automatically, create clear return policy page with free returns.
